Implementation details
- Increased Fighter torso readability with richer chest panel layering, bevel-style highlight bands, flank shading, and stronger fauld separation.
- Upgraded pauldrons with layered shoulder plate construction, highlight passes, and stronger asymmetry while preserving correct front-view composition.
- Added segmented arm/gauntlet treatment so sword/shield arms read as armored limbs instead of simple line strokes.
- Reworked Fighter leg rendering with clearer thigh volume, knee plate shapes, greaves, sabaton/boot cap definition, and stronger metal/cloth separation.
- Improved sword rendering with fuller-like detailing, a clearer crossguard, better grip readability, and stronger bevel highlights.
- Improved shield rendering with layered rim/interior facets, central emblem highlight, and better material separation without allowing the shield to dominate the body silhouette.
- Refined belt/tabard/front detail passes to support a denser premium procedural mesh look while keeping performance stable.
