Dream Realms V0.15.04 - Dark Woods Density Boundary Pass

Summary
- Reduced Dark Woods hostile density by roughly 50% from the mob spawn owner.
- Kept spawn coverage distributed across the full zone instead of deleting large regions of content.
- Added a dense, visible, impassable tree wall around the entire Dark Woods map perimeter.
- Added an inner foliage fringe so the zone edge reads as dense forest, not an invisible wall.
- Boundary trees are real collision/pathing blockers, so players and mobs treat them as solid obstacles.
