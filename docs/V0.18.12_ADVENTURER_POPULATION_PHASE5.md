## What Phase 5 delivers
- **Mercenaries (abstract).** When a party lacks a **tank** or **healer**, the
  richest member who can afford it hires a merc of that role to fill the gap; a
  wealthy lone adventurer hires a bodyguard of the role it lacks. Mercs are stored
  as record data (no entity is spawned for an off-screen adventurer) but use the
  **real `DR.MERC_ROLES` hire costs** so the economy matches the player's.
- **Upkeep.** Each merc costs a daily upkeep (30% of hire cost). At dawn every
  adventurer pays its mercs; **if it can't afford them, mercs leave** until it can â€”
  exactly the spec's "if upkeep cannot be paid, the mercenary leaves."
- **Supplies & repair.** Adventuring adventurers pay for supplies before heading out
  and a daily repair cost scaled by gear score.
- **Selling gear.** On a level-up, an adventurer realizes gold from selling the gear
  it replaced.
- **"Poor bots gather instead."** An adventurer below the poverty threshold gathers
  resources (income, low spend) rather than paying to go adventuring â€” a
  self-correcting floor that keeps the economy from collapsing.
