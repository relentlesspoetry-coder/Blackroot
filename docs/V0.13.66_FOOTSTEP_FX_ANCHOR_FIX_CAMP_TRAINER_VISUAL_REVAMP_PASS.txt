Footstep FX Anchor Fix
- Added a shared actor foot-contact anchor resolver for humanoid/class-family actors.
- Terrain dust and grass interaction effects now use foot-contact screen offsets instead of spawning at the actor root/body origin.
- The resolver accounts for movement direction, facing, class-model profiles, robe/cape-heavy silhouettes, and alternating step side.
- Effects remain terrain-owned and do not alter actor shadows, hitboxes, collision, or movement behavior.

Grass Interaction FX Anchor Fix
- Grass-blade interaction events now receive the same foot-contact anchor offsets used by dust puffs.
- Existing terrain material checks, cooldowns, and visual effect behavior are preserved.

Boar Model Polish / Revamp Work
- Refined the dedicated boar renderer again to reduce circular/oval placeholder reads.
- Strengthened front-heavy silhouette, shoulder hump, neck/head connection, wedge head, snout/nose disk, tusk placement, hoof grounding, belly contour, and body-plane shading.
- Preserved charge, attack, hit, death, rare/elite/named variant, Old Tusk Briarback, health/nameplate anchor, and corpse-rendering behavior.

Starting Camp Layout / Prop Cleanup
- Rebuilt starting camp placement with better tent spacing, clearer walk lanes, cleaner prop clusters, and less cramped NPC access.
- Removed/replaced awkward MERC-style signage, ground flags, and placeholder camp clutter from the generated starter camp cleanup path.
- Kept vendor, trainer, quest, Spirit Healer, and camp access behavior intact.

Class Trainer Visual / Model Replacement
- Trainer NPC definitions now include class-family renderer metadata such as className, playerClass, classId, rendererId: classTrainer, visualRole: class_trainer, and trainerVisualVariant.
- NPC rendering now prioritizes matching class-family trainer models for trainers before falling back to generic human NPC rendering.
- Trainer interaction, hover/click behavior, nameplates, and placement are preserved.

Gameplay Systems Preserved
- Movement, combat, targeting, threat ownership, boar behavior, trainer interactions, camp navigation, Spirit Healer, save/load, map systems, party/bot/pet systems, inventory, paper doll, item comparison, bag-to-ground drag/drop, seamless terrain/water, half-block rendering, and Esc menu behavior were not intentionally changed.
