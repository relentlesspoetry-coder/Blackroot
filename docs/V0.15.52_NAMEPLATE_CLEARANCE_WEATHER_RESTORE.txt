Dream Realms V0.15.52 — Nameplate Clearance Weather Restore Pass

Fixes:
- Nameplates and attached HP/threat/meditation bars now sit above the actor/model head with a clear vertical gap.
- Applies to players, bots, mercenaries, pets, NPCs, and mobs.
- Shared nameplate stacking now resolves upward first and prevents the bottom of the nameplate stack from being pushed into the actor model.
- Weather rendering is restored for rain, snow, fog, thunder/lightning, sand/dust storms, and ambient dust.
- Weather world overlays and particles now render in screen space after the world camera transform, preventing camera-transform/weather visibility regressions.
- Performance/adaptive modes no longer fully suppress weather visuals outside caves.

Changed owning files:
- render/entity-renderer.js
- systems/weather-system.js
- systems/performance-verification-system.js
- systems/adaptive-performance-system.js
- game.js
- core/config.js

No gameplay/content changes:
- No class, spell, mob, loot, quest, dungeon, economy, XP, mana, cooldown, DPS, or combat-balance values were changed.
