# V0.18.14 — Adventurer Population, Phase 7 (reputation & recurring characters)

Seventh slice of the Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System (plan:
`docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`). The reputation that's been quietly accumulating
now **surfaces**: adventurers gain standing, earn titles, and become recognizable
recurring names.

## What Phase 7 delivers
- **Standing tiers.** Reputation maps to a standing: **Unproven → Known → Respected
  → Renowned → Famed → Legendary** (thresholds 0/20/40/75/150/300).
- **Earned titles.** An untitled adventurer who rises to **Renowned** earns a
  class-appropriate epithet (e.g., a Fighter becomes "the Bulwark", a Cleric
  "Lightbringer"), chosen deterministically. The three hand-authored seed titles
  (Arlen "the Hunter", Mira "the Kind", Sable "the Grey") are preserved.
- **Milestone word-of-mouth.** Rising into a notable tier (Renowned+) posts a chat
  line — "Word spreads of Doran the Bulwark — now Renowned across the Dark Woods."
  It's **edge-triggered** (once per tier), and the rank is persisted so nothing
  re-announces after a reload.
- **Recurring recognizable names.** `/who` shows each adventurer's standing, and a
  new `adventurerRenownList()` surfaces the most renowned — so over a campaign a few
  adventurers clearly stand out.

## Mechanics (`systems/adventurer-population-system.js`)
- `REP_TIERS` + `standingFor(rep)`; `NOTABLE_RANK` (Renowned); `EPITHETS` per class
  + `earnedTitleFor(rec)` (deterministic pick).
- Record fields: `standing`, `standingRank` (persisted, drives edge detection),
  `titleEarned`.
- `updateAdventurerStanding(rec)` — recompute tier; on a rank increase into a
  notable tier, earn a title (if untitled) and announce once. Called from the
  abstract sim whenever a dormant adventurer's reputation ticks up.
- `/who` shows `Rep N [Standing]`; `adventurerRenownList(limit)` returns the top
  names with standing.

## Verified (in-browser, 0 console errors)
- Initial standings from seed rep are correct (Arlen 41 → Respected, Bram 34 →
  Known, Orin 16 → Unproven).
- An untitled Fighter (Doran) crossing into Renowned **earned "the Bulwark"**, was
  flagged `titleEarned`, reached `Renowned` standing, and posted both the title and
  word-of-mouth log lines.
- An already-titled adventurer (Arlen) crossing into Renowned **kept "the Hunter"**
  (no new title).
- Re-running at the same rank **did not re-announce** (edge-triggered).
- `/who` renders `Rep 90 [Renowned]` and the earned title; the renown list surfaces
  the top names.
