Dream Realms V0.12.21 — Layered World Depth Stage 1 Pass

Source brief
- The world-depth request called for strict layering, tile bevels, deterministic variation, wall-face details, ground decals, prop contact shadows, lighting texture, and foreground/depth groundwork.

Implemented in this pass
- Terrain renderer now generates deterministic tile variants through hash2D/getTileVisualVariant.
- Floor tiles receive bevel highlights, lower-edge shadows, chipped corners, pebbles, cracks, moss, dust, stain, and decal overlays.
- Exposed wall side faces now use vertical gradients and added detail passes: seams, cracks, moss/root streaks, rubble at base, ledge lips, and broken edge detail.
- Object renderer now draws contact shadows under common props before drawing the prop itself.
- Sun shafts now vary width and opacity, add soft floor impact patches, and draw drifting deterministic dust motes.

Deferred
- Stage 2 sparse prop placement and foreground occlusion objects.
- Stage 3 room-specific atmosphere templates.
- Stage 4 room identity templates for shrine/spider/flooded/boss/treasure rooms.
