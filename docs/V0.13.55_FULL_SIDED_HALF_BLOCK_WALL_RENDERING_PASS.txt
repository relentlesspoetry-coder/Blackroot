Dream Realms V0.13.55 — Full-Sided Half-Block Wall Rendering Pass

Systems Changed
- render/terrain-renderer.js
  - Added a centralized half-block terrain exposure resolver for north, east, south, and west sides.
  - Updated structural-pass eligibility so cached ground tiles still emit depth faces when any exposed side borders lower terrain, water, or map void.
  - Reworked depth-face descriptor generation to use explicit edge geometry for northFace, eastFace, southFace, and westFace instead of relying on one-off bottom-point conditionals.
  - Added systematic corner joins for adjacent exposed side faces.
  - Added corner occlusion caps to keep the V0.13.53 ground-over-depth-face seam fix intact at exposed corners.

Full-Sided Half-Block Wall Rendering
- Raised/walkable terrain now resolves all four cardinal exposure directions independently.
- North-exposed terrain draws a north-facing vertical depth face.
- South-exposed terrain draws a south-facing vertical depth face.
- East-exposed terrain draws an east-facing vertical depth face.
- West-exposed terrain draws a west-facing vertical depth face.
- Land bordering water is treated as a visual exposure even when legacy map data stores land and water at the same elevation.
- Map bounds are treated as recessed/void exposure for edge tiles.

Edge / Corner Adjacency Handling
- The resolver now treats lower terrain, water, cave-wall-to-floor boundaries, and void as exposed boundaries where appropriate.
- Corner joins are generated for:
  - north + east
  - east + south
  - south + west
  - west + north
- Joins close anti-aliased gaps on isolated platforms, narrow bridges, convex terrain points, concave inlets, and stepped shorelines without adding a global dark outline.

Chunk Composition Changes
- Terrain chunk caching remains intact.
- Neighbor sampling from prior terrain-cache passes is preserved.
- Structural eligibility now uses lightweight adjacency checks before cached chunk drawing, avoiding a full-map redraw or a duplicate render loop.
- Depth faces still render in the terrain-depth-underlay phase.
- Cached water/ground surfaces still draw above the depth underlay.
- Ground occlusion lips and corner caps still draw above the cached ground surface.

Gameplay Systems Preserved
- This is a rendering-only pass.
- Collision, pathfinding, spawn validation, water blocking, mob placement, loot placement, quests, combat distance, ranged auto attacks, Purify, Resurrection, autonomous bots, pets, dungeon logic, save/load, and Esc menu behavior were not intentionally changed.
- V0.13.54 auto-attack speed tuning was preserved.
