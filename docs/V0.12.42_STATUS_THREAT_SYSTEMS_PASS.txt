Dream Realms V0.12.42 - Status Threat Systems Pass

Owned systems added:
- systems/status-effect-system.js
- systems/threat-system.js

Status Effect System:
- Owns buff/debuff duration, remaining time, stacks, max stacks, stat modifiers, control tags, DoT ticks, HoT ticks, source attribution, colors, and serialization.
- Supports hostile and beneficial effects.
- Supports control tags such as slow, root, stun, and silence for future ability expansion.
- Preserves compatibility with existing entity.addBuff callers while routing new behavior through the status owner.
- Replaces the previous combat-owned periodic damage loop.

Threat / Aggro System:
- Owns per-enemy threat tables.
- Damage creates source-owned threat.
- Pet damage creates pet-owned threat.
- Mercenary damage creates merc-owned threat.
- Healing creates group healing threat across nearby hostile enemies.
- Taunt forces a temporary target and bumps the taunter above current threat.
- Enemy target selection now prefers threat targets, then proximity fallback.
- Threat decays and clears when actors die, leash out, or enemies reset/respawn.

Gameplay wiring:
- Combat damage and healing feed threat.
- Enemy AI uses threat target selection.
- Guardian mercenary uses a periodic taunt.
- Status effects render on player, target, and mercenary HUD trays.
- Character saves persist active player status effects.

Verification target:
- No duplicate Game.prototype owners.
- No bottom-of-file hotfix/cascade wrapper.
- Syntax check all JS files.
- HTML load order includes status/threat before combat.
