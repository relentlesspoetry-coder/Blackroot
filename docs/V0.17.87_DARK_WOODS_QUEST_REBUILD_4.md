# V0.17.87 — Dark Woods Quest Rebuild IV (Stone Hedge)

**Type:** Content + engine (quests)
**Previous version:** V0.17.86
**New version:** V0.17.87

Adds the **Stone Hedge Clearing** ([17]–[20]) and three new mechanics: night-gating, a sequence
puzzle, and a defend race. All four quests are given by **Selene Glasswhisper** (`npc_enchanter_trainer`).

---

## New engine (`systems/quest-system.js`)

- **Night-gating** — `isNight()` reads `game.getWorldLightState().nightStrength > 0.6`. A task with
  `nightOnly: true` only advances after dark (`advanceObjective` skips it by day with a throttled hint),
  and a `nightOnly` defend refuses to start by day.
- **Sequence puzzle** — interact points with `sequenceId` / `seq` / `sequenceTotal` are re-usable (never
  consumed) and must be activated in order (`activateSequenceStone`). A wrong stone resets the sequence and
  spawns a wisp pack (`mob_duskwisp` via `spawnQuestWaveEnemy`); completing the order advances the interact
  objective. No dependency on the puzzle-system.js window UI.
- **Defend race** — a defend task with `holdSeconds` must clear all waves within the limit (measured from
  encounter start); otherwise it resets.

## Content

- **[17] The Order of Stones** — PUZZLE. `requiredConditions: itemOwned item_tarnished_coin` (the level-2
  coin from [02] keys a level-7 puzzle). 8 standing stones around the ring (210,250) woken **sunwise from
  the north**; a wrong stone summons wisps. Reward: 1300 xp + Wardstone Fragments + a Duskwisp Charm.
- **[18] Wispbloom Under Moonlight** — HARVEST/DAILY, `nightOnly` gather of `herb_wispbloom`. The first
  quest the day/night cycle gates.
- **[19] Lumen** — BOUNTY, `nightOnly` kill of `mob_lumen_wisp` (spawns at 210,256). Reward-choice picker:
  **Lumenshard** (relights lanterns — loops back to [01]) or **Wispflame Focus**.
- **[20] What the Gap Lets In** — DEFEND/RACE/INVESTIGATION. `nightOnly` defend at the ring, 5 waves,
  `holdSeconds: 180`. Seeds the Gloamroot arc — the ring's gap, which faces the road, was *left* open.

New items: `item_lumenshard`, `item_wispflame_focus`, `item_wardstone_fragment`.

## Verification (in-browser, port 8899)

- **[17]**: a wrong-order stone spawns 3 wisps and resets the sequence; waking all 8 in order solves the
  quest.
- **[18]**: a wispbloom gather makes **no** progress with `nightStrength = 0` and progresses at
  `nightStrength = 0.9`.
- **[20]**: the ring defend refuses to start by day and starts at night with `holdSeconds = 180`, wave 0
  spawned.
- No console errors.
