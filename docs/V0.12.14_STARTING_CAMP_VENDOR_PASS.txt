Dream Realms V0.12.14 — Starting Camp Scale and Vendor Pass

Diagnosis
- Starting camp props were visually close to character scale, making tents/buildings read like small decorations.
- NPCs needed to stay at player scale, while camp structures needed a separate prop scale pass.
- No dedicated starting camp vendor existed for selling inventory items.

Fix
- NPCs/players/mercs continue sharing the same humanoid character scale.
- Added large camp tent and vendor stall rendering paths that scale much larger than characters.
- Added Quartermaster Brann as a vendor NPC near the starting camp.
- Added vendor sell window and sell-value calculation for bag items.
