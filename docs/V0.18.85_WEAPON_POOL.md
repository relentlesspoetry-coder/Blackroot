# V0.18.85 — Dark Woods Weapons (Roadmap Item 4 B)

Itemization moves from armour to **weapons**. Ten new main-hand weapons spread across the class
archetypes (`DR.CLASS_ARCHETYPES`, `data/loot.js`) so every class gains at least one. Weapons differ
from armour: `type: weapon`, `slot: weapon`, a real `damage: {min, max, speed}` range, `twoHanded`
on two-handers, and `armor: 0`. Same rarity spread (2 white / 5 green / 3 blue) and audit-guarded
sourcing.

**Class coverage** (every class gets ≥1): Fighter/Paladin/Warden → sword/mace/maul/reaver; Rogue/
Assassin → daggers (+bow); Ranger → shortbow; Bard → sword/daggers; Wizard/Enchanter/Summoner → wand/
staff; Necromancer/Summoner → bonewand/staff; Cleric/Druid/Shaman → mace/staff.

Two class-oriented pieces (Hollowbone Wand, Hollowsteel Reaver). Faster weapons have lower `damage.speed`
(dagger 1.6) and slow two-handers higher (reaver 3.0, maul 3.4). Stats use only real STAT_KEYS.

## Sourcing (every weapon obtainable — audit-guarded)

- **Vendor (3):** camp general store (`systems/event-system.js`, `SHOP_ITEM_LISTS`) — Gloomiron
  Sword, Briar Dagger, Oaken Staff.
- **Loot (6):** `data/loot-tables.js`, appended beside the cape drops at slightly lower weapon chances
  — wolves (Stalker's Shortbow), briar boars (Gravebell Mace), duskwisps (Duskwisp Wand), ashroot
  horrors (Warden's Maul), named rares (Hollowsteel Reaver), common chest (Hollowbone Wand).
- **Craft (1):** `data/crafting.js` `recipe_weapon_briartusk_fang` — a fang-dagger cut from a briar
  tusk, campfire (profession tailoring/bonework).
