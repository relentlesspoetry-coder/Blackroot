Portal changes:
- Portal visual scale increased to 1.4x while preserving the bluish-purple spinning vortex, orbiting particles, rim strokes, ground rune, and screen flash.
- Player activation radius increased to align with the larger portal footprint.
- T-key portal creation now anchors the portal at the player location, with the previous forward offset kept only as a fallback if the current tile cannot host the portal.
- Existing behavior remains intact: one active pair, T replaces the old pair, first entry sends the player to Dead Lantern Camp, return portal sends the player back once, then the link collapses.

Follower changes:
- Party bots follow only when they are actually in the player's bot party set.
- The hired mercenary follows portal travel even when treated as an auxiliary follower rather than a party-size slot.
- The owned summoned pet/familiar follows portal travel.
- Followers are moved in the same portal use as the player and do not consume additional uses.
- Followers are placed around the arrival point with spacing and walkability checks so they do not stack directly on the player or portal.
- Bots, mercenaries, pets, mobs, and NPCs still cannot independently trigger the portal; activation remains player-only.

Save compatibility:
- Account, character, and world save schemas are unchanged.
