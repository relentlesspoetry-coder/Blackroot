Dream Realms V0.12.41 - Weather System Pass

Implemented:
- New owned weather runtime: systems/weather-system.js.
- Biome definitions: temperate forest, desert, tundra, mountain, swamp, plains, cave.
- Season modifiers: spring, summer, autumn, winter.
- Graduated weather definitions:
  - Clear / partly cloudy / overcast.
  - Light fog / fog / heavy fog.
  - Light rain / rain / heavy rain.
  - Thunderstorm with light rain / rain / heavy rain.
  - Light snow / snow / heavy snow.
  - Light sandstorm / sandstorm / heavy sandstorm.
- Climate state: temperature, humidity, wind speed, elevation.
- Biome/elevation/weather gating:
  - Caves suppress active weather visuals.
  - Snow requires allowed biome/elevation/temperature.
  - Sandstorms require sandstorm-enabled biomes.
  - Low humidity suppresses fog/rain/thunder.
  - Extreme cold suppresses rain/thunder.
- Weather transitions and weighted automatic weather selection.
- Weather HUD under the minimap with debug cycle control.
- Save/load persistence through world saves and character runtime saves.
- Day/night integration through TimeCycle light modifiers instead of competing sky owners.

Rendering:
- Weather-tinted sky overlay.
- Foreground rain, snow, fog, sand, and storm particles.
- Weather ground fog and visibility haze.
- Thunder lightning flash and bolt rendering.
- Weather-adjusted night darkness, fog density, firefly suppression, dust suppression, star visibility, and sun-shaft strength.

Ownership:
- No cascade patch layer.
- No late setTimeout wrappers.
- Weather logic is owned by systems/weather-system.js.
- Time-of-day lighting remains owned by systems/time-cycle-system.js and consumes weather modifiers through a direct runtime query.
