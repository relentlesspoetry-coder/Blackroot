# V0.18.54 — Silk Web Caverns: the tiny spiders crawl like spiders

Your feedback: *"the tiny spiders just spin around in circles on the walls instead of crawling around like spiders, also the spiders on the floor slide around instead of crawling."*

Showcase: [V0.18.54_spider_gait.png](V0.18.54_spider_gait.png) — top row is one spider at increasing `walkPhase` (the legs step through the gait cycle); bottom row is the spider facing several directions.

## Root cause
`drawTinySpider` (`render/object-renderer.js`) drew a **static pose** — the 8 legs were fixed. So:
- **Floor spiders**: translating a static shape looks like **sliding**.
- **Wall spiders**: they were oriented by a *continuous time-based rotation* (`wallNow * 0.0009 + ph`) on a smooth sinusoidal loop path, so they **spun in circles** in place.

## The fixes
### 1. A real leg gait (`drawTinySpider`)
Added a `walkPhase` argument. The 8 legs now step with an **alternating-tetrapod gait**: each leg swings fore/aft (`reach`) and lifts on its forward swing (`lift`), out of phase with its neighbours (`sin(walkPhase + legIx*1.9)`), plus a small body bob. `walkPhase = 0` yields a natural static pose, so callers that don't animate are unchanged.

### 2. Floor crawlers step only while moving
`dungeonSystem.updateSilkCritters` (`systems/dungeon-system.js`) accumulates a per-critter `c.walk` phase **only while the critter actually moves** (`c.walk += speed·dt·16`), and `renderSilkCritters` passes `c.walk` to `drawTinySpider`. So the legs step as it crawls and hold still when idle — no more sliding. (It already faced its velocity via `atan2(vy, vx)`.)

### 3. Wall spiders walk-and-face (`drawSilkCavernWallWebs`, `render/terrain-renderer.js`)
Rewritten. Each spider now **walks back and forth across the web** — a ping-pong triangle-wave sweep (`cyc = (now·speed + phase) % 2`, `tri = cyc<1 ? cyc : 2-cyc`), so it walks to an edge, turns, and walks back — and **faces its direction of travel** (the web's `ta→tb` "across" angle, flipped when it turns), with the stepping gait. No more continuous spin.

### 4. Nest spiders step while orbiting
`drawGiantSpiderNest` now passes a `walkPhase` to the on-nest spiders so their legs step as they orbit (they already faced their tangent/orbit direction).

No gameplay changes.
