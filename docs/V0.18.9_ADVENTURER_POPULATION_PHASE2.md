# V0.18.9 — Adventurer Population, Phase 2 (promote/demote bridge)

Second slice of the Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System (plan:
`docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`). This is the **two-tier core**: the bridge
between lightweight persistent *records* (Phase 1) and full-AI `BotPlayer`
entities.

## What Phase 2 delivers
Nearby non-squad adventurer records **materialize** into real `BotPlayer` entities;
they **demote** back to data when the player moves away. The 8 squad records bind to
the existing `BOT_START_PROFILES` bots (always materialized) so they're never
doubled. This is what keeps frame cost flat no matter how big the population grows —
only a handful of records are ever live entities at once.

## Mechanics (`systems/adventurer-population-system.js`)
- **World positions** — `ensureAdventurerWorldPositions()` gives each non-squad
  record a deterministic, walkable overworld anchor (golden-angle scatter at
  adventuring distance from the camp, via the existing `findBotSpawnPoint`). Squad
  records get their position synced from their live bot.
- **Promote** — `promoteAdventurerRecord(rec)` builds a bot profile from the record
  and spawns it via the existing `spawnBotPlayer()` at the record's anchor, tagging
  the entity `adventurerRecordId = rec.id` and `worldAdventurer = true`.
- **Demote** — writes live state back to the record (`syncAdventurerRecordFromBot`)
  and removes the entity via the existing `removeBotPlayer()`.
- **Bridge loop** — `updateAdventurerMaterialization(dt)`, called after
  `updateBotPlayerSystem` in the frame update, **throttled to 0.5 s**:
  - squad records → bind to their live bot + mirror position/hp/activity/level/gold
    (never promoted/demoted);
  - materialized distant records → demote when the player is beyond **34 tiles** or
    changes zone;
  - dormant distant records → promote when the player is within **26 tiles** of the
    anchor (the 26/34 gap is hysteresis against spawn/despawn thrash).
- **Save safety** — `materialized`/`botId` are runtime links; they reset to
  `false`/`null` on load (the bot doesn't exist yet) and the bridge re-binds /
  re-promotes as the player moves.
- `/who` now marks materialized adventurers with a `●`.

## Verified (in-browser, 0 console errors)
- All 4 distant records get walkable positions; the 8 squad records bind to their
  live bots (`squadRecordsBound = 8`; the separate `classbot-*` roster is correctly
  ignored).
- **Promote on approach:** Arlen & Mira materialize near spawn; moving the player
  onto Doran's anchor spawns Doran.
- **Demote when far:** moving the player away removes Arlen & Mira (bot count 16→14,
  `materialized=false`).
- **No double-spawn:** repeated passes keep Doran at exactly 1 entity.
- **Squad bots untouched** by the bridge (stay at 8).
