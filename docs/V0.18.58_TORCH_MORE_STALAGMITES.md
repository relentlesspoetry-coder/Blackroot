# V0.18.58 — a real torch + way more stalagmites

Your screenshot showed the dungeon torch rendering as a brown stick topped by a solid yellow triangle — it read as an upward arrow/cursor, not a torch. Plus: way more stalagmite columns around the dungeon.

Showcase: [V0.18.58_torch_stalagmites.png](V0.18.58_torch_stalagmites.png) — two torches (left) and the stalagmite variants (small / large-webbed / column / webbed-column).

## 1. The torch is now an actual torch
`drawTorch` (`render/object-renderer.js`) was a line + a `fillPoly` triangle. Rebuilt as a real torch:
- a **tapered wooden handle** with a grain shadow;
- a **pitch-soaked wrapped head** — a dark bundle with 3 binding bands and charred embers;
- a soft **warm radial glow**;
- a **3-layer flickering flame** (outer orange `#ff5f18`, mid amber `#ffb43c`, inner bright `#ffe9a8`) drawn as teardrop béziers that **sway** with a two-sine flicker;
- **rising sparks**, and a contact shadow.

## 2. Way more stalagmites
`placeSilkCeilingWebsAndJunk` (`systems/dungeon-system.js`) — stalagmite counts raised and columns spread to every room:
| variant | before | now |
|---|---|---|
| small | big?2:1 | **big?6:3** |
| large (webbed) | big-only ×1 | **big?3:1 in every room** |
| floor-to-ceiling column | big-only ×1 | **big?4:2 in every room** (some silk-draped) |

The **tunnel pass** now also drops stalagmites (including columns) along the corridors on their own lattice, so the passages aren't bare rock.

`drawCaveStalagmite` gained a **tile-position seed fallback** (like the columns/piles in V0.18.56), so the many new stalagmites vary instead of all sharing `seed=1` and rendering identically.

No gameplay changes.
