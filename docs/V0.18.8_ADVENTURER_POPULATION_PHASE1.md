# V0.18.8 — Adventurer Population, Phase 1 (persistent registry)

First shippable slice of the **Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System** (plan:
`docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`; spec: `Blackroot_Adventurer_Bot_AI_System.txt`).
Decisions locked with the user: **full arc, phase by phase**, targeting **a dozen
recognizable recurring adventurers**.

## What Phase 1 delivers (additive only — no behavior/AI change)
The persistent **data layer**: a registry of a dozen adventurer *records* that exist
independent of whether a full `BotPlayer` entity is materialized for them (the
two-tier design from the plan). This phase seeds the roster, persists it, and makes
it visible. It does **not** yet change how bots spawn or behave, and runs **no**
simulation — that's Phase 2 (promote/demote bridge) and later.

## New file: `systems/adventurer-population-system.js`
- `AdventurerRecord` shape: `id, name, race, className, role, personalityId, level,
  xp, gold, reputation, title, homeTown, gearScore, activity, state, region,
  questRef, hpPct, materialized, squadBot, guildId, lastDawnDay` (schema-versioned,
  migration-safe `normalizeRecord`).
- Curated cast of **12** (`SEED_ADVENTURERS`): the first 8 mirror the existing squad
  bots (`BOT_START_PROFILES` — Bram, Talia, Riven, Luma, Elowen, Orin, Fenn, Varek)
  so Phase 2 can fold them together; plus 4 new distant adventurers — **Arlen the
  Hunter** (Ranger) and **Mira the Kind** (Cleric) from the spec's examples, **Doran**
  (Fighter) and **Sable the Grey** (Wizard).
- Game methods: `initAdventurerPopulation` (idempotent seed; keeps a restored
  population), `adventurerRecord(id)`, `adventurerPopulationList`,
  `serializeAdventurerPopulation` / `restoreAdventurerPopulation` (migration-safe,
  backfills missing cast members), `adventurerWhoCommand`, `adventurerPopulationSummary`.

## Wiring
- Registered in `_script_order.json` + the HTML script list (after
  `bot-player-system.js`), installed at `AdventurerPopulationSystem.install(Game)`.
- Seeded on world entry in `start()` (right after `ensureBotPlayers()`).
- Persisted in the save under `runtime.adventurerPopulation` (serialize next to
  `botPlayers`, restore next to `restoreBotPlayerState`).
- New **`/who`** chat command lists the roster (name/title, race, class, level,
  reputation, current activity), sorted by reputation.

## Verified (in-browser, 0 console errors)
- On world entry the registry holds **12** records; a spot-checked record (Arlen the
  Hunter — Human Ranger Lv 9, Rep 41, gearScore 108, home Lantern Camp) is correct.
- Serialize → 12 records; restore round-trip rebuilds from data (a mutated field
  reverts to the saved value).
- `/who` prints a 12-line ranked roster (Arlen 41 → Mira 37 → Bram 34 → …).
