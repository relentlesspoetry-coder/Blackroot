Dream Realms V0.13.17 - Inventory Item Tooltip Completion Pass

Scope:
- Rebuilt item tooltip generation around one structured builder in systems/inventory-system.js.
- Tooltip content now includes item name, canonical rarity, item type, slot, weapon/armor/bag details, stats, requirements, effects, sell value, stack data, and flavor text.
- Rebound tooltips across inventory slots, equipped gear, bag bar bags, corpse loot, vendor buy/sell rows, merc gear, and bot inspection/trade gear.
- Added viewport-safe tooltip positioning and mobile long-press inspection without interfering with dragging or double-click equip.
- Removed reliance on native title hover text for item slots once the shared tooltip binder attaches.

Ownership:
- systems/inventory-system.js owns item tooltip builder/renderer/binder.
- systems/event-system.js owns shop row item tooltip integration.
- systems/loot-system.js owns corpse loot row item tooltip integration.
- systems/bot-player-system.js and systems/mercenary-system.js expose item nodes to the shared tooltip binder.

No cascade patch layer, late wrapper, setTimeout patch, or duplicate tooltip manager was added.
