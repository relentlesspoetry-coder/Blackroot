## The design (user's picks)

**Soft enrage** ‚Äî after a **45s grace**, boss damage climbs **+8% every 10s, capped at +80%.** Rising
pressure that punishes stalling/kiting; no instant wipe.

**HP-threshold phases:**

| HP | effect |
|---|---|
| ‚â§ 50% | +12% damage, +20% attack speed ‚Äî *"grows enraged"* |
| ‚â§ 25% | +24% damage, +40% attack speed ‚Äî *"turns desperate"* + a **one-time burst** |

The 25% burst: **summon adds** if the boss has a summon ability, otherwise a big **telegraphed
Desperate Strike** ‚Äî which reuses the `enemy_aoe` queue, so it's **dodgeable and interruptible** like
every other ground AoE (the telegraph/interrupt systems pay off again).

## Architecture ‚Äî one funnel

`updateBossEscalation(game, dt)`, called each frame from `update()`, recomputes `_bossDamageMult` and
`_bossAttackSpeedMult` from **combat time + current HP** ‚Äî so a **healed boss de-escalates**, and the
phase *announce* + 25% *burst* are one-time high-water latches.

**Consumption:**
- **Damage** ‚Äî multiplied in `getEnemyAutoAttackRawDamage` (auto-attacks + data abilities via `hit()`)
  **and** `scaleEnemyAbilityDamage` (string abilities, DoTs). Every enemy-damage source covered.
- **Attack speed** ‚Äî divides the swing delay at both `attackTimer = getEnemySwingDelay(...)` sites.

**Gating** ‚Äî `isBossEnemy` (dungeonBoss/miniboss/boss/named/rank-boss). **Normal and elite mobs never
escalate** (multiplier stays 1). **Resets** on `respawn()` and on leash/deaggro (fresh fight).

stuns decisive, and the boss burst dropped straight into all three.
