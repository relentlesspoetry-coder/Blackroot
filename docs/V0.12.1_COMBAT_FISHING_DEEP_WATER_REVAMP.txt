Dream Realms V0.12.1 — Combat, Fishing, and Deep Water Revamp

Baseline: corrected V0.12.0.

Changes:
- Removed loaded turn-based combat runtime.
- Combat now uses slow real-time EverQuest-style auto-attack pacing.
- Attack key starts auto-attack on the current/nearest target; movement remains available during combat.
- Enemy, mercenary, and pet attack timers are slowed to make combat more readable.
- Damage-over-time effects now tick over real time instead of enemy turns.
- Fishing hotspots are removed from runtime play; players can cast into any valid nearby water tile.
- Fishing line origin is anchored to the visible rod tip.
- Aquatic fish remain visible in rivers, ponds, lakes, and cave pools.
- Water tiles now render as deep water wells/drop-offs, using 5-12 block visual depth.
- Terrain side faces render for north/east/south/west edges so rotated camera views keep complete block sides.

Ownership:
- Combat changes live in combat-system.js and entity AI files.
- Fishing changes live in fishing-system.js and player fishing render helpers.
- Terrain/deep-water changes live in terrain-renderer.js and water-depth assignment in world/cave/swimming systems.
- No bottom-layer hotfix or wrapper scripts were added.
