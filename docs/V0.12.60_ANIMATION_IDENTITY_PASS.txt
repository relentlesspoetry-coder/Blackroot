Summary
- This pass adds stronger motion/posture identity without adding a new gameplay system.
- It keeps all class, merc, and NPC rendering on the shared humanoid framework.

Class animation identity
- Fighter: heavier grounded motion, shield brace, footfall dust.
- Rogue: lower stealth posture, faster hands, subtle movement afterimage.
- Cleric: calmer prayer posture, blessing pulse, halo idle/cast read.
- Summoner: ritual orbit and floating focus motion.
- Necromancer: restrained eerie motion and dark mote idle/cast flavor.
- Bard: performance pose shaping.
- Druid: rooted casting / nature meditation shaping.

Merc/NPC animation identity
- Smith: hammer-spark idle.
- Cook: stirring/steam cue.
- Fisher: rod movement cue.
- Merchant: coin glint.
- Bard Trainer: musical note idle.
- Scout: low motion trace.
- Warden/Guardian: grounded guard stance.
- Healer/Field Cleric: healing glow.
- Arcane Adept: orbiting arcane focus.
