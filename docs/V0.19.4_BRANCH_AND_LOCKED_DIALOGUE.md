# V0.19.4 — Locked-State Dialogue (State 7) + The Rurik Branch (State 6)

## The violation

`systems/quest-system.js` `unavailableQuestReason()` returns bare system strings — and the caller
**speaks them as the NPC**:

```js
game.log(`${source.name}: ${this.unavailableQuestReason?.(pick.id) || 'That quest is locked.'}`);
```

So all **8 condition-gated quests** answered the player with **"Requirements not yet met."** in their
giver's voice. Most damningly: **Road Warden Cael** — whose patrol you may have just murdered to join
Rurik — replying to you with a developer string.

State 7 exists precisely to forbid this: *"provides contextual hints without exposing internal
prerequisite flags."*

## The fix

New authored **`lockedText`**, preferred by `unavailableQuestReason` **only** in the
`requiredConditions` branch:

```js
return quest.lockedText || 'Requirements not yet met.';
```

The **prerequisite** (`"Complete X first."`) and **level** (`"Requires level N."`) branches are
deliberately left alone — they carry genuinely useful information, and blanketing them with
`lockedText` would lose it. *Verified: Thalen still correctly says "Complete Offerings first."*

## State 6 for free — the fork's distinct responses

The Rurik fork's two gated dailies **are** each side's distinct response to your choice:

| quest | giver | gate |
|---|---|---|
| `quest_bandit_bounties` | Road Warden Cael | needs `rurik_dead` |
| `quest_fence_work` | The Bandit Fence | needs `rurik_allied` |

So authoring their `lockedText` delivers the roadmap's *"distinct responses for each selected
outcome"* through the game's **existing gating**, without inventing a new mechanic:

> **Cael:** "Bounties are for a broken band, and Rurik still has his hands on that ruin. Bring me his
> end and I'll open the purse — and if you've been drinking with him instead, don't come to me for
> warden coin."

> **The Fence:** "I trade with Rurik's people, and you are not one of them. Get his word on you and
> we'll talk. Come at me as a warden's errand-runner and the only ledger you'll be in is the one I
> keep for debts."

## State 6's other half — consequences before you commit

- **The Bargain** now names the three wardens on the north road, *"men you've likely shared a fire
  with"*, and that there is no road back to the wardens afterwards.
- **The Fall of Rurik** warns his people will remember your face, no fence in that ruin will ever deal
  with you again, and that *"the wardens pay for a broken band. Rurik pays for a kept one. Choose
  which coin you want."*

Both fork quests also had their `inProgressText` rewritten from **raw objective restatement** (*"Kill
Rurik the Fallen at the ruin."*) into character — state 3 requires in-progress dialogue *"remain in
character rather than repeating raw objective text."*

## Authored

`lockedText` for all **8** condition-gated quests: Cael's bounties, Fence Work, The Bargain, The Order
of Stones, The Fourth Expedition Member (permanent loss), Anti-Venom Supply (cold vats), The Long
Root, Follow the Blood. `validateQuestDialogue` now also scans `lockedText` (**7** spoken fields).

## Verification — and a false pass I caught

The first test reported `"Requires level 6."` — because the **level check fires before** the condition
check, so the `lockedText` path was never reached. That was a **false pass**. Re-tested with a
level-12 stub so the *condition* gate is what fails:

- All **8** gated quests speak in character.
- **Zero** say "Requirements not yet met."
- Both fork refusals read as intended.

Plus: `node --check` clean; JSON valid; **0 console errors**; suite **PASS** with 0 dialogue issues and
descriptor warnings still 2.
