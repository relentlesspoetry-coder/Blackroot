Dream Realms V0.12.36 — Bard Meditation and Fishing Animation Pass

Renderer ownership
- render/bard-procedural-model.js now owns bard-specific meditation/fishing pose logic.
- render/entity-renderer.js now forwards meditation/fishing state into the bard renderer.

Included animation states
- Meditation: seated bard pose, crossed legs, aura ring, rising restorative orbs, lute resting across lap.
- Fishing: bard-specific hand placement for rod handling, fishing rod overlay, fishing line, reel spool, and continuous rod tip anchor output.
- Existing perform/song state remains intact.

Implementation notes
- Meditation and fishing are drawn directly by the bard renderer rather than falling back to the generic humanoid renderer.
- Instrument rendering is suppressed while fishing so the bard does not appear to play and fish simultaneously.
- Nameplate anchor is lowered during meditation to match the seated bard silhouette.
