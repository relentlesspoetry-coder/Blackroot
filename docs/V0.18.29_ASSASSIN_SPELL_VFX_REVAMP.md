# V0.18.29 — Assassin spell-animation revamp

User:

> "Alright starting with the Assassin class. Let['s] revamp their spell
> animations, get rid of all the generic spell animations and add some actual
> depth to all the spell animations so they better behave as the spells they are."

First class of a planned per-class VFX pass.

## Problem
The Assassin resolver (`systems/spell-system.js` `resolveAssassinRevampSpell` +
`applyAssassinTrap`) only gave **five** spells bespoke VFX (Throwing Knife, Light
Crossbow Shot, Poison Dart, Marked for Death, Tripwire). Everything else fell back
to generic effects:
- every other **damage** spell → `spawnBolt` (a generic purple bolt);
- **AoE** (Fan of Knives, Death Box), **traps** (Poison Snare, Springblade) and
  **all self-buffs** → `spawnRing` (a generic ring).

## What changed

### `render/effects-renderer.js` — per-weapon silhouettes
Two data tables (`ASSASSIN_PROJECTILE_KINDS`, `ASSASSIN_IMPACT_KINDS`) give each
weapon kind its own look, and the projectile/impact renderers are now
table-driven:

| kind | in flight | on impact |
|---|---|---|
| `knife` / `blade` | steel core **tumbling end-over-end**, dashed motion line | steel sparks |
| `crossbow` | light bolt + smoke wisp | standard burst |
| `heavyBolt` (piercing) | thick, fast, bright **speed-streak** | **punch-through** shards fly out the far side |
| `venomBolt` | green bolt shedding **venom motes** | green splash + smoke |
| `executionBolt` | crimson bolt with a lethal **glint** | crimson **execute sigil** + glow |
| `poisonDart` | green dart, venom motes | green splash |

New renderers:
- `drawAssassinSelfBuffEffect(e)` — one function, five `style`s: `reload` (steel
  ratchet spins shut + bolt seats), `silentstep` (cold shadow smoke), `shadowfuse`
  (violet fuse-lines whip out), `venomcoat` (venom sweats up), `ambush` (crimson
  readiness sigil).
- `drawAssassinTrapFieldEffect(e)` — Death Box: a mechanical kill-zone of tension
  wires with snapping blade nodes over the spell radius.

### `systems/effects-system.js` — spawners + a reusable `delay`
- `spawnAssassinProjectileVfx` / `spawnAssassinImpactVfx` are keyed per kind
  (speed + tint).
- New: `spawnAssassinSelfBuffVfx(style)`, `spawnAssassinTrapFieldVfx(center, radius)`,
  `spawnAssassinFanVfx(source, {count, angle, …})` (a cone of tumbling blades),
  `spawnAssassinBurstVfx(kind, source, target, count)` (N staggered bolts).
- Added a general effect **`delay`** field: `updateEffects` holds an effect (no
  `t` advance) until its delay elapses, and `drawEffect` skips delayed effects —
  so Repeater Burst's bolts stagger instead of firing on the same frame.

### `systems/spell-system.js` — routing, generic fallbacks deleted
The resolver now maps every spell to its weapon (`ASSASSIN_PROJECTILE_KIND`) and:
- crossbow spells fire the aim reticle first;
- **Repeater Burst** → 5-bolt staggered burst;
- **Ricochet Blade** → primary blade + a real bounce blade to the second target;
- **Fan of Knives** → a fanned cone of blades aimed at the targets + per-hit blade impacts;
- **Death Box** → the mechanical trap-field zone + per-enemy blade impacts;
- **all traps** → wire deploy VFX (Poison Snare tints green + a venom pulse,
  Springblade adds a steel blade burst);
- **self-buffs** → per-style flourishes.

The generic `spawnBolt` / `spawnRing` fallbacks were **removed** from the Assassin
paths entirely.
