**Type:** Content + engine (quests)

Adds the whole **Bandit's Fall** region ([11]‚Äì[16]), built around Blackroot's first real branching

---

## New engine (`systems/quest-system.js`)

- **`quest.onComplete` effects** (`applyQuestOnComplete`) ‚Äî `setVariable` writes **global** event-system
  variables (`bandit_hostility`, `rurik_dead`, `rurik_allied`), read back by `requiredConditions`
  `{ type:'variable', name, op, value }` on gated quests. (Global variables, not per-node self-switches, so
  they work as character-wide flags.)
- **`quest.spawnOnAccept`** (`spawnQuestOnAccept`) ‚Äî spawns mobs when a quest is accepted (reuses
  `spawnQuestWaveEnemy`); [14a] spawns 3 on-demand Warden Patrols to kill.
- **`DR.QUEST_BRANCHES`** (`checkBranchTriggers`) ‚Äî the `choose` verb. Discovering a branch's `poi` shows a
  **must-pick** choice modal (`showQuestChoiceModal`) when its `requiredConditions` pass; otherwise the
  `fallbackQuestId` is offered with `fallbackLog`. It only fires once a resolution is actually acceptable
  (level/conditions), so an under-level player at the ruin never gets a dead prompt. Resolved once.
- **Reward-choice picker** (`promptRewardChoice`, gated by `rewards.playerChoice: true`) ‚Äî a genuine
  player-facing picker that replaces the class-auto choice. Retrofit to **[08] Old Tusk**.
- **`foundAtPoi`** gate on auto-offer HIDDEN quests ‚Äî [13] is found in the world (at the watch post), not
  auto-accepted at spawn.

## Content

- **[11] Blood on the Road** (Cael): discover `poi_bandit_ambush_remains` + kill 6 Bandit.
- **[12] Cut the Supply Lines** (Cael, prereq [11]): interact 4 `bandit_supply_cache` at the ruin.
  `onComplete: bandit_hostility += 4` ‚Äî quietly closes the [14a] bargain (hostility ‚â• 3). The player is
  never told; they find out by living with it.
- **[13] The Lookout's Last Watch** (HIDDEN, `foundAtPoi: poi_collapsed_watch_post`): discover the watch
  post + recover the torn ledger. Seeds the Gloamroot zone arc.
- **[14] Rurik's Offer** ‚Äî the BRANCH (`DR.QUEST_BRANCHES`, triggered by discovering `poi_bandits_fall`):
  - low hostility ‚Üí choice modal ‚Üí **[14a] The Bargain** (kill 3 Warden Patrols ‚Üí `rurik_allied=1`) XOR
    **[14b] The Fall of Rurik** (kill Rurik ‚Üí `rurik_dead=1` + reward choice).
  - hostility ‚â• 3 ‚Üí no bargain; **[14b]** offered directly.
  Both auto-complete (Rurik is not a turn-in NPC).
- **[15] Bandit Bounties** (Cael, `requiredConditions: rurik_dead ‚â• 1`) ‚Äî repeatable, warden path.
- **[16] Fence Work** (new `npc_bandit_fence` @ 216,70, `requiredConditions: rurik_allied ‚â• 1`) ‚Äî
  repeatable, pays **more gold** than [15]. The two dailies are deliberately unequal; neither is correct.

New assets: mob `mob_warden_patrol`; NPC `npc_bandit_fence`; 4 items ‚Äî `item_torn_lookout_ledger`,
`item_bandit_lockpicks`, `item_ruriks_fallen_blade` (first unique weapon), `item_fences_ledger`
(vendor-discount flavour; not yet wired to prices). Cael now offers [11], [12], [15].

## Verification (in-browser, port 8899)

- Low-hostility discovery of the ruin shows the 2-option "Rurik's Offer" modal; picking "Hear his bargain"
  accepts [14a] and closes the modal.
- After completing [12], `bandit_hostility = 4`; re-triggering the branch offers **only** [14b] (bargain
  closed) ‚Äî the invisible consequence works.
- **Bargain path**: [14a] spawns 3 Warden Patrols; killing them auto-completes it, sets `rurik_allied=1`,
  grants lockpicks; **[16]** becomes available and the Fence resolves as its quest source; **[15]** stays
  locked.
- **Fall path**: killing Rurik auto-completes [14b], sets `rurik_dead=1`, shows the reward picker; picking
  grants Rurik's Fallen Blade; **[15]** becomes available, **[16]** stays locked.
- No console errors.
