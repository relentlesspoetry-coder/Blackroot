Dream Realms V0.12.33 — Wolf-Specific Procedural Renderer Pass

Source
- Implemented from the uploaded WolfProceduralModel drop-in specification.

Implementation
- Added render/wolf-procedural-model.js.
- Added the module to the HTML load order before render/entity-renderer.js.
- Added isWolfEnemyActor() and drawWolfEnemyModel() to render/entity-renderer.js.
- drawEntity() now routes wolf-type enemies to drawWolfEnemyModel() before drawEnemySprite().
- Dead wolf enemies route to the dedicated wolf corpse renderer instead of the generic corpse blob.
- Live wolves skip the generic small enemy shadow because the wolf renderer draws a wider quadruped shadow.

Visual identity
- Cave-gloom wolf palette with blue-green tint.
- Long quadruped body with shoulder/chest mass.
- Direction-aware lowered head, muzzle, ears, eyes, and tail.
- Four readable legs with paws.
- Attack snarl and collapsed corpse pose.
- 8-direction profiles for east, southeast, south, southwest, west, northwest, north, and northeast.
