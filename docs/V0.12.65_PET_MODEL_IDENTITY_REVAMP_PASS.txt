Dream Realms V0.12.65 — Pet Model Identity Revamp Pass

- Improve summon / idle / walk / attack / hit / death readability without adding bottom-of-file patch layers.

- Necromancer pet now renders as a bone-servitor / bone-knight silhouette with clearer skull, ribs, armor, ragged cloth, and necrotic glow.
