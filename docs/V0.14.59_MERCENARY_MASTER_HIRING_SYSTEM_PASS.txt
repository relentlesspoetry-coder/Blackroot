Dream Realms V0.14.59 — Mercenary Master Hiring System Pass

- Added the Mercenary Master NPC in the Dark Woods hub near trainers and quest services.
- Created a Mercenary Hiring UI with available mercenary name, class, level, role, and hire cost.
- Hiring now checks player gold, existing active mercenary state, and party capacity.
- Hired mercenaries join the party automatically when a slot is available; otherwise they remain active followers outside the party.
- Added same-window dismissal for active hired mercenaries.
- Added Mercenary Master dialogue: "Looking for capable swords, magic, or stealth? My mercenaries never disappoint... for a price."
- Added right-click NPC interaction support by routing world picks through the NPC runtime.
- Updated the Mercenary Master visual profile with a rugged tunic/leather jerkin, pauldrons, coin pouch, commander cape, contract prop, and officer identity distinct from playable class armor.
