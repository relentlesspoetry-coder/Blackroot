# V0.18.59 — Silk Web Caverns: stalactites + size variety, and a dark, foggy night

Your request: more stalagmite varieties — both hanging from the ceiling and on the ground, and different sizes (big, small, large, extra-large, extra-small); make the cave darker like night; and add a slight layer of fog slowly rolling across the ground.

Showcase: [V0.18.59_stalactite_sizes.png](V0.18.59_stalactite_sizes.png) — bottom row: ground stalagmites xs→xl; centre: webbed floor-to-ceiling column; right: hanging stalactites xs→large.

## 1. Stalagmite / stalactite size variety
`drawCaveStalagmite` (`render/object-renderer.js`) was rebuilt around a shared **tapered-spike** helper that draws from a wide base to a point either **up or down**. It now supports:
- **five ground sizes** — `xs`, `small`, `medium`, `large`, `xl` (graduated width + height tables);
- **hanging stalactites** — `obj.hang` anchors the spike ~200 px above the tile and points it **down** from the ceiling, in the same five sizes;
- the floor-to-ceiling **`column`** (stalagmite + meeting stalactite).

Placement (`systems/dungeon-system.js` `placeSilkCeilingWebsAndJunk`):
- every room scatters all five **ground** sizes + columns, **plus a fringe of hanging stalactites** (xs / small / medium / large);
- the **tunnel** pass drops both ground stalagmites (incl. columns) and hanging stalactites along the corridors on their own lattices.
- some are silk-draped, and each varies (position-seeded from V0.18.58).

## 2. Night darkening + ground fog
New `game.renderSilkCavernAtmosphere(ctx)` (`render/object-renderer.js`), called from `game.js` right before `drawVignette()`. It runs **only in the `silk_web_cavern`**:
- **Night**: a cool dark tint (`rgba(8,6,16,0.4)`) over the whole view + a radial **vignette** (to `rgba(3,2,9,0.55)` at the edges) so the cave reads as night and enclosed.
- **Fog**: a slight layer of pale fog — 8 soft wisps drawn with a `screen` (lightening) blend — **slowly drifting** across the lower/mid screen, with a gentle vertical bob.

No gameplay changes.
