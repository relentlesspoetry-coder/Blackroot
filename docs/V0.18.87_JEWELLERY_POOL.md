# V0.18.87 — Dark Woods Jewellery (Roadmap Item 4 C)

The jewellery slots get a full pool of 10 across **all four jewellery slots** — and the **earring
slot, which had no items before**, is now filled. Jewellery is `type: accessory`, `armor: 0`, and
class-agnostic (`allClasses`) utility. Same rarity spread (2 white / 5 green / 3 blue), audit-guarded
to 0 unsourced.

## Paired slots

Rings and earrings each have two equip slots (`ring1/ring2`, `earring1/earring2`). An item declares
`slot: 'ring'` or `slot: 'earring'` and the equip resolver in `systems/inventory-system.js` (lines
~525–671) drops it into the first free slot. Verified: compiled ring instances resolve to `ring1`,
earrings to `earring1`.
