## New engine (`systems/quest-system.js`)

- **`worldEffect` onComplete type** + `sealDarkWoods()` / `relightAllLanterns()` — the capstone's permanent
  world change. `relightAllLanterns()` scans `overworldObjects` and sets **every** `lanternPost` to
  `intact` / `lit`. On [25] completion the whole Dead Lantern Trail is relit for good — the level-1 quest
  [01] finally ends at level 10 — and `dark_woods_sealed = 1` is set. The completion log delivers the
  reveal (the rot is contained, not cured; what made it came from elsewhere — there is a road).

(Everything else reuses shipped verbs: discover, deliver-to-POI, kill, `spawnOnAccept`, defend with
`holdSeconds`, the player reward-choice picker, and `requiredConditions {type:'level'}`.)

## Content

- **[24] Offerings** (Elder Thalen Mossroot / `npc_druid_trainer`): discover `poi_root_shrine`, then
  **deliver** one `item_blackroot` (deep), one `item_wispbloom` (stones), one `item_thornberry` (bramble)
  at the shrine — a full lap of the zone's three regions. Reward `item_grove_token`.
- **[25] The Long Root** — CAPSTONE (`requiredConditions: level ≥ 10`, prereq [24]): discover `poi_the_rot`
  + kill the **Ashroot Elder** (`spawnOnAccept` spawns `mob_ashroot_elder` at the rot) + **defend** the
  Root Shrine (100,220) through **6 waves** with `holdSeconds: 300` while Thalen seals the rot.
  Reward-choice picker: **Heart of the Long Root** (the zone's best charm) or **Thalen's Seal**.
  `onComplete`: `dark_woods_sealed = 1` + `worldEffect sealDarkWoods` (permanent lantern relight + reveal).

New items: `item_grove_token`, `item_heart_of_the_long_root`, `item_thalens_seal`. Thalen now gives [24] and [25].
