Dream Realms V0.13.52 — Edge Outline Cleanup + Organic Terrain Boundary Pass

SYSTEMS CHANGED
- render/terrain-renderer.js owns the new cached organic edge geometry for material transitions, paths, shoreline occlusion, and the legacy non-cached fallback.
- The existing terrain/water chunk architecture, composition phases, invalidation, and gameplay map data remain unchanged.
- Runtime, save, world, README, HTML, and version metadata identify V0.13.52.

ORGANIC MATERIAL BOUNDARIES
- Grass/dirt, forest/path, camp/grass, stone/soil, cave, ruin, and other walkable material boundaries now use shared cubic contour geometry.
- Broad low-contrast strokes interleave both neighboring material colors, replacing isolated transition blobs and exposing less of the square source boundary.
- A restrained inner highlight keeps roads and paths readable without restoring a hard outline.
- World-coordinate control points and endpoint offsets are deterministic; consecutive boundary segments share exact endpoints.
- Transition geometry is built into the existing ground elevation canvas once per dirty chunk.

SHORELINE OUTLINE CLEANUP
- Rectangular shore-cap fill strips were removed.
- Straight tile-length underside contact lines were removed.
- Shore caps now use a curved dark underside, material-colored land cap, and subtle highlight along one organic contour.
- Curves shift slightly over the logical water boundary to preserve V0.13.51 ground-over-water-wall occlusion.
- Rounded curve joins reduce half-block stairs at straight banks, corners, inlets, ponds, and rivers.
- Ground and water remain visually distinct, and shoreline depth faces still render before cached surfaces.

FALLBACK / HARD EDGES
- The legacy drawWaterEdgeHalfBlock helper was removed and replaced by a restrained curved shore-depth fallback.
- Legacy material edge strips now use curved low-alpha strokes instead of rectangular half-tile polygons.
- Cave walls, cliffs, elevation drops, and other real blockers keep their structural faces and stronger readability.

PERFORMANCE / COMPATIBILITY
- No new render loop, per-frame boundary scan, full-map redraw, blur filter, or overlay system was added.
- Organic boundaries are cached inside the existing 16×16 terrain chunks and share camera culling, signatures, editor invalidation, bounded retention, and seam bleed.
- Logical terrain/water cells remain the sole source for collision, pathfinding, water rules, spawn/placement checks, combat distance, loot, quests, and dungeon validation.
- Existing saves load without migration; cache geometry remains transient.
- Seamless ground/water, shore depth layering, actors, props, autonomous parties, pets, targeting, inventory, paper doll, currency, Purify, resurrection, ranged attacks, Silk Web Cavern, Dark Woods, and Esc save/exit remain intact.
