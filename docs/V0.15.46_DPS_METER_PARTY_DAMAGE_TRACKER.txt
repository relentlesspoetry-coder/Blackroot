Dream Realms V0.15.46 — DPS Meter Party Damage Tracker Pass

Feature Added:
- Real-time DPS meter with centralized combat damage reporting.

Root integration:
- systems/combat-system.js now reports final applied damage from Game.prototype.damageEntity through Game.prototype.recordDamageEvent.
- DoT ticks are detected through options.isPeriodic.
- Pet damage is attributed to the pet owner.
- Mercenary damage is attributed to the hiring owner.
- Player, bot, and party-member direct damage is tracked as owner damage.
- Dungeon fresh runs and player death can reset DPS totals.

UI:
- Added systems/dps-meter-system.js.
- Ctrl+D toggles the DPS meter.
- Settings includes DPS Meter controls.
- DPS meter window is movable and resizable.
- Window modes: Last 10s, Last 30s, Current Fight.
- Reset button clears current totals.
- Party mode ranks party members by rolling DPS.
- Solo mode shows player total plus direct/DoT/pet/merc breakdown.
- Per-target totals are shown in the meter footer.

Performance:
- Damage is reported through one centralized event path.
- The meter aggregates damage events and updates the display on a fixed interval, not on every tick.

No gameplay/balance changes:
- No class, spell, mob, loot, quest, dungeon, economy, XP, mana, cooldown, or damage-balance values were changed.
