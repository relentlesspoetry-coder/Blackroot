# V0.18.16 — Adventurer Population, Phase 9 (tuning & scale) — ARC COMPLETE

Final slice of the Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System (spec:
`Blackroot_Adventurer_Bot_AI_System.txt`; plan: `docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`).
This phase verifies performance, balances the rates for real-play pacing, and
closes out the whole living-world build.

## Real-play time scale
`secondsPerGameDay = 14400` → **4 real hours per in-game day** (1 in-game hour = 10
real minutes). All balancing below is judged against that.

## Performance (the architectural claim, verified)
Timed the full adventurer update stack (world events + daily scheduler + abstract
sim + promote/demote bridge), **forcing every method to its expensive path each
iteration**: **0.015 ms per full-stack call** (2,000 iterations, 30 ms total). That's
~0.09% of a 16.7 ms frame, and it does not scale with anything problematic — only
the ~12 records and at most one active event are ever touched, and in real play the
methods are throttled and only do work when in-game hours tick. Frame cost is flat,
exactly as the two-tier design promised.

## Balance (checked against real pacing)
- **Progression, economy, death are all bounded and believable.** A 12-in-game-day
  (48 real hour) real-time-paced campaign evolved the four world adventurers to L20,
  with **Arlen the Hunter reaching Renowned**, the others Respected, each holding a
  2-mercenary retinue, gold healthy and **soft-capped (1.3k–2.4k)**, and **9 deaths**
  distributed correctly by role (glass-cannon DPS died most, tank/healer least) —
  all recovered.
- **Event cadence tuned.** Events were too sparse for a normal session (one start
  per ~6 real hours). The post-event gap was shortened (6–16 in-game hours ≈ 1–2.7
  real hours); because events are long-lived, **the world now has an active
  happening ~54% of the time** — steadily alive without spam.
- Everything else (`SIM`, `ECON`, `DEATH`, `REP_TIERS`, response chance, promote/demote
  radii, travel speed, population size) was confirmed sensible and left as-is. All
  remain one-line constants for future re-tuning.

## The whole system (Phases 1–9)
A curated cast of **a dozen recurring adventurers** that live independently of the
player:
1. **Registry** — persistent records, saved with the game, viewable with `/who`.
2. **Promote/demote bridge** — nearby records materialize into full world bots and
   demote back to data; squad records bind to the existing squad bots. Flat frame
   cost.
3. **Daily scheduler** — each dawn they roll an activity, form parties, and travel.
4. **Abstract sim** — dormant adventurers gain XP/levels, gold, gear, reputation and
   spend/recover HP off-screen.
5. **Economy & mercenaries** — hire and pay mercs, buy supplies, repair, sell gear;
   gather when poor. Bounded net-flow.
6. **Death & recovery** — fall at critical HP, visit the spirit healer with a
   resurrection penalty, recover; healers cut the risk. Nobody is permanently lost.
7. **Reputation & recurring characters** — standing tiers, earned class titles at
   Renown, word-of-mouth. Recognizable names emerge.
8. **World happenings & social** — caravans, beast hunts, calls-to-arms that
   adventurers answer; campfire gatherings.
9. **Tuning & scale** — flat perf verified; rates balanced for real play.
