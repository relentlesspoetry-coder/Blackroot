Dream Realms V0.12.38 - Core Stats Scaling Pass

Implemented:
- Armor mitigation now uses diminishing returns with a 68% hard cap.
- Cooldown reduction now uses the same diminishing-return curve family with a 40% hard cap.
- Physical crit chance, magic crit chance, physical crit damage, and magic crit damage now have explicit hard caps.
- Class attributes now drive derived combat stats:
  - Strength: physical damage and physical crit damage.
  - Dexterity: physical crit chance, speed, and minor physical output.
  - Stamina: HP, defense, and armor rating.
  - Intelligence: mana, magic damage, and magic crit damage.
  - Wisdom: healing output, magic crit chance, mana, and support scaling.
- Player equipment, training bonuses, buffs, combat damage, healing, CDR, and character sheet display now use the same stat model.

Ownership:
- core/stats.js owns formula constants and stat math.
- systems/inventory-system.js owns equipment/stat aggregation.
- systems/combat-system.js owns final outgoing/incoming damage and healing resolution.
- systems/spell-system.js owns spell cooldown application and spell damage typing.
