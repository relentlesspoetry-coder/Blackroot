Dream Realms Modular Pass 48 - Water / Fishing / Cave Foundations

Scope:
- Swimming / underwater traversal foundation.
- Hidden underwater cave/dungeon entrances.
- Moving anti-AFK fishing hotspots.
- Water visual polish.
- Fishing profession starter tools, fish item drafts, fish loot behavior.
- Cooking link through cooked fish recipes.
- Gathering/mining default nodes and tool requirements.
- Cave-pool water and underwater dungeon draft expansion.

Runtime controls:
- Walk into water to swim.
- Press U while swimming to dive/surface.
- Press E while underwater near bubble/cave indicators to enter a hidden underwater dungeon entrance.
- Press F near visible fishing ripples/bubbles to fish.
- Press G near herb/mining resource nodes to gather/mine.

Implementation notes:
- Fishing no longer succeeds from arbitrary nearby water. It requires an active hotspot.
- Hotspots relocate on a timer, preventing safe AFK fishing at one static tile.
- Resource nodes now seed default Dark Woods herbs and Mossfang Cave herb/mining nodes.
- Mining requires a pickaxe; fishing requires a rod.
