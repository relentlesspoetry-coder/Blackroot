Role behavior:
- Guardian: tank stance, taunt, shield strike, high threat generation.
- Field Cleric: emergency heal, normal heal, ranged support, healing priority for player/merc/pet.
- Arcane Adept: ranged DPS, spacing control, mana-aware arcane bolts.
- Blade Scout: melee assist, flank positioning, faster blade strikes.

Command behavior:
- Follow: stays with owner and only reacts to direct threats.
- Assist: supports the player target or nearby active threats.
- Guard: protects the player and intercepts nearby hostile enemies.
- Passive: follows only and does not attack.
- Attack: aggressively engages the player target or nearest valid enemy.
- Meditate: rests to full HP/mana when out of combat.

Recovery behavior:
- Mercenaries are now marked downed instead of silently falling into an unusable state.
- Downed mercs recover near the player after combat is safe and the recovery timer completes.
- Revive resets combat state, restores partial HP/mana, returns the merc to assist unless passive was active, and refreshes party/UI state.

Cascade status:
- No bottom-of-file override layer.
- No delayed setTimeout repair layer.
- No monkey-patch wrapper.
