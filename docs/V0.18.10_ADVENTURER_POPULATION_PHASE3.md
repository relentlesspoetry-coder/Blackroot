# V0.18.10 — Adventurer Population, Phase 3 (daily activity scheduler)

Third slice of the Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System (plan:
`docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`). The world now **churns day to day**: every
in-game dawn each adventurer picks what to do, groups up, and sets off.

## What Phase 3 delivers
- **Daily activity roll** — each in-game dawn every adventurer rolls a primary
  activity from the spec distribution: **rest 15% / craft 10% / gather 15% / solo
  20% / small-group 25% / large-group 15%**.
- **Party formation** — world adventurers who roll a group activity are chunked
  into parties (small = 3–4, large = 5–6) that share a single destination. Squad
  records stay with the player and never join a travelling party.
- **Destinations & travel** — each activity picks a destination: in-town activities
  (rest/craft) stay at the camp; adventuring ones pick a walkable point at a
  distance scaled by activity reach **and** the adventurer's level (deeper regions
  for higher levels). Dormant (off-screen) records then **walk toward** that
  destination over the day, so where you find each adventurer shifts between days.
- **Materialized behaviour** — when a world adventurer materializes (Phase 2), its
  bot `behaviorGoal` is set from today's activity (rest/craft → camp, gather → road
  patrol, solo/small → questing, large → dungeon-prep).

## Mechanics (`systems/adventurer-population-system.js`)
- `ACTIVITIES` distribution + `pickActivity` (weighted); `PARTY_SIZE`.
- Record fields added: `activityId`, `groupId`, `travelTarget` (+ `activity`,
  `activityLabel`, `state`).
- `rollAdventurerDailyActivities()` — rolls activities, forms parties among
  non-squad records, assigns shared party / per-record destinations via
  `pickAdventurerDestination()`.
- `updateAdventurerDailySchedule(dt)` — called in the frame update before the
  materialization bridge: detects the in-game **day rollover** (`worldTime.day`
  via `getWorldTimeInfo`) and re-rolls; otherwise advances dormant distant records
  toward their `travelTarget` (5 tiles/s, off-screen data only — live bots own
  their own movement).

## Verified (in-browser, 0 console errors)
- All 12 adventurers get a valid activity each roll; the distribution shows the
  expected variety.
- Parties form; **every party shares one destination** and **squad records are
  never placed in a travelling party** (checked across 8 days of rolls).
- The 4 distant records get travel targets; a dormant record (Doran) measurably
  walks toward its target (distance 70 → 44 over ~5 s) and arrives.
- The in-game day rollover re-rolls (lastDawnDay advances); squad records carry an
  activity for display but no travel target.
