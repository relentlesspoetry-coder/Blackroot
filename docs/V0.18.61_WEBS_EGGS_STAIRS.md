# V0.18.61 — Webs, Egg Piles & Cave Stairs (part 2 of the request)

Showcase: [V0.18.61_webs_eggs_stairs.png](V0.18.61_webs_eggs_stairs.png) — top: two orb-web ground hazards, then large / small / royal egg piles; bottom: two natural cave staircases.

## 1. Ground web redesign + 30-second vanish
The webs you read as "the webs the spiders shoot" are the `webHazard` "Thick Webbing" ground traps. They used to draw as **concentric ellipse ripples** (your screenshot).

- **Redesign** (`render/object-renderer.js` `drawWebHazard`): now a real **orb-web splat on the ground** — iso-squashed **radial spokes** + a **capture spiral** + bright **silk beads** at the junctions. On break the spiral gaps and the spokes tear with rising silk puffs; when a target is caught, a countdown ring floats above it.
- **30s vanish** (`systems/dungeon-system.js` `updateSilkWebControlObjects`): each web is stamped with `vanishAt = now + 30000` the first time it's seen, and removed (`row[x] = null`) ~30 s after it appears if it hasn't already been triggered/broken. (The trigger/slow behaviour is unchanged until then.)

## 2. Bigger, more varied egg piles
New `drawEggPile` (object type `eggPile`): a **silk-nest mound** packed with **varied-size** spider eggs, some **hatched** (a torn husk + a dark hole, sometimes a hatchling), draped in **webbing** with **1–2 guard spiders** crawling on it. **Large** and **small** sizes, **royal** (pink) on floor 3, and **position-seeded** so each pile is different. `placeSilkCeilingWebsAndJunk` scatters large piles in the brood/egg rooms + open caverns and small ones elsewhere.

## 3. Natural cave stairs
`drawDungeonStairs` now routes the Silk Web Cavern to a new **`drawCaveStairs`**: rough hewn **stone treads** + shadowed **risers** (irregular, position-jittered) descending into a **dark radial-gradient cave mouth**, flanked by **rough rock shoulders** and draped **cobwebs** — replacing the old flat parallelogram steps + "DOWN" text. Other dungeons keep their existing stairs.
