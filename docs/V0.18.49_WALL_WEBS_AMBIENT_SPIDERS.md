# V0.18.49 — Silk Web Caverns: wall-web varieties + free-roaming ambient spiders

Completes the "spiders live here" request — the two items deferred from V0.18.48.

Showcase: [wall-web varieties + crawlers/splat](V0.18.49_wall_webs_and_crawlers.png).

## 1. Wall-web varieties
> "The webs on the wall also need to look like spider webs and they need different varieties like some stretching down to the floor, some with eggs on them, some with tiny spiders crawling on them."

`drawSilkCavernWallWebs` (`render/terrain-renderer.js`) already drew heavy base silk sheeting on every wall face. On top of that, each wall tile now picks **one variety** via a per-tile hash (`hash2D(x*2, y*3, 9190)`):

- **(a) floor-stretch curtain** (`< 0.3`): a translucent sheet + heavy drapes that hang from the top of the wall face **down past the bottom edge to the floor** in front of it.
- **(b) egg sacs** (`0.3–0.55`): 3–6 clustered egg sacs on the web sheet.
- **(c) crawling spiders** (`0.55–0.78`): 1–3 tiny spiders on the web (via the shared `drawTinySpider`).
- the rest keep just the base heavy sheeting.

So the walls read differently tile-to-tile instead of one uniform drape.

## 2. Free-roaming ambient spiders
> "place tiny little spiders all over the dungeon just crawling around they don't fight or anything but if the baby steps on them they just get squished and die."

A new **lightweight, purely-cosmetic critter system** — deliberately **not** enemies (no AI, HP, combat, threat, or nameplate; not in `game.enemies` / `game.entities`, so they never affect targeting, aggro, or objective counts):

- **Spawn** — `spawnSilkCritters(game, floor, rooms, map, objects)` (`systems/dungeon-system.js`), called from `generateSilkWebCavernFloor`, seeds ~**26–34** crawlers on walkable floor tiles into `game.silkCritters`. Regenerated on each floor entry.
- **Update** — `dungeonSystem.updateSilkCritters(dt)` (wired into the dungeon update loop next to `updateBossSeal`): random-walks each crawler across the floor, **bouncing off walls** (a move onto a non-`CAVE_FLOOR` tile is rejected and the heading turns). Any crawler a **friendly actor** (`[player, merc, pet, ...botPlayers]`) steps within **0.42 tiles** of is **squished** — marked dead, shows a brief splat, then culled after ~0.42 s. Cleared when you leave the cavern.
- **Render** — `game.renderSilkCritters()` (`render/object-renderer.js`), called from `game.js` **right after** `renderSilkWebFloorOverlay` — so it draws in the floor layer and party members visibly walk **over** the crawlers. Live ones draw via `drawTinySpider` (oriented to their heading); dead ones draw as a fading squish splat.
