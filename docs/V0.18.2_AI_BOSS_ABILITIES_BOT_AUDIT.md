# V0.18.2 — AI overhaul V: boss summons/mobility + bot-AI audit

Closes the two follow-ups from V0.18.0/V0.18.1: the remaining stubbed enemy ability
kinds, and a review of bot skill-use AI.

## Part 1 — the stubbed ability kinds (`entities/enemy.js`)

The V0.18.0 executor left six kinds as no-ops (`default: return false`). Now implemented
in `executeDataAbility`, all gated by the existing range/cooldown logic in `tryDataAbility`,
and all damage stays `getEnemyAutoAttackRawDamage × damageScale (+ damageBonus)`:

| kind | behaviour |
|---|---|
| `summon_adds` / `phase_adds` | `Enemy.summonMobAdds` spawns `summonCount` temporary adds around the caster via `game.spawnQuestWaveEnemy` |
| `charge` / `lunge` | dash `advance` tiles toward the target, then strike (lunge adds a small AoE + status at the landing) |
| `blink` | teleport to just short of the target, strike + a **Flicker** slow |
| `retreat` | burst away from the target while still facing it |
| `cocoon_prison` | hard root (`moveSpeedMultiplier: -1`) + light AoE damage |

**Summon resolution** — `Enemy.resolveSummonAddId`: uses `ab.summonMob` / `baseType.summonMob`
if set, else picks a **family-appropriate weak, non-elite** mob from `DR.MOB_DRAFT_BY_ID`
(spider bosses → `mob_thorn_widow`; otherwise the weakest, `mob_rotling`). Adds spawn at
level-1, are tagged `isSummonedAdd`, and don't self-respawn.

**Result:** every boss/miniboss now uses its full designed kit — notably **The Egg-Heart**
(a summon-only miniboss) actually summons now instead of just auto-attacking.

**Verified (in-browser):** summon spawned 3 adds (resolved `mob_rotling`); `charge` at proper
range dealt 44 dmg and closed distance; `blink` teleported adjacent + 38 dmg + Flicker;
`cocoon_prison` rooted the player; `retreat` backed the mob away. A mixed 4-caster
exotic-ability fight ran with **0 console errors**, and the abilities are attack-gated (no
per-frame cost).

## Part 2 — bot skill-use AI: audited, **not changed**

The bots were already comprehensive, so — per "don't break working systems" — they were
reviewed and left as-is:

- All **14 classes** have tuned `BOT_CLASS_AI_PROFILES` (role, `preferredRange`, heal /
  emergency thresholds, an ordered `spellPriority`).
- `chooseBotClassSpell` / `botSpellMatchesPriority` already select emergency-heal (<35%),
  heal (<70%), revive, cure, crowd-control, mana restore, pet-heal/buff, summon, defensive
  and group-buff — with mana + cooldown + line-of-sight gating.
- Bots already **kite / position**: the combat positioner repositions ranged bots when
  `d < preferredRange - 1.25` (too close) and closes melee bots to range.

**Verified:** no orphaned spells (every class spell's `kind` is reachable by its priority
list); in live combat the Cleric healed injured allies while casters bolted enemies; and
the game's own `auditClassBotAiStability()` returns `ok: true` (14 classes, no missing
profiles / spellbooks / cooldowns).

**Optional future work:** DPS bots have no self-preservation (they facetank at low HP with
no healer nearby), though they respawn. Not a bug — left as a tuning choice.
