Performance changes
- Added a frame-safe camera projection cache for worldToScreen(), cameraDepth(), screenToWorld(), cameraCenter(), and beginWorldCameraTransform().
- Avoided repeated camera center/player tile elevation/yaw sin-cos/viewport-half calculations in hot projection paths.
- Reused pooled render queue records for tile, water-depth, terrain-depth, object, entity, effect, and damage renderables instead of allocating new queue objects every frame.
- Precomputed viewport dimensions, projection zoom, cull padding, and viewport cull limits outside the inner tile loop.
- Culled offscreen effects and floating damage text before inserting them into the sorted world render queue.
- Reduced threat-system allocation churn by replacing filter/Object.entries/Object.keys hot paths with direct loops and lightweight table scans.
