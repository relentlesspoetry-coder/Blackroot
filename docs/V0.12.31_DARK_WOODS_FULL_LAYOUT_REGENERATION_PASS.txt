Dream Realms V0.12.31 — Dark Woods Full Layout Regeneration Pass

Design Intent
- Dark Woods was rebuilt from scratch rather than patched on top of the prior terrain layout.
- The flat-floor-only constraint was canceled before implementation. Elevation/half-block terrain is now allowed when it is intentional and tied to landmarks, ridges, ruins, cave shelves, or shorelines.

World Generation
- Replaced the generateMap() terrain body with a fresh authored/procedural map pass.
- New terrain families include safer near-camp forest, mid-zone dark woods, deep underbrush, stone ridges, ruin shelves, marsh pockets, and water bodies.
- Added a main winding creek, a south-west side stream, and several ponds/lakes.
- Added intentional elevation via radial danger gradient, stone ridges, ruin shelves, cave shelves, and landmark terrain.

Layout
- Dead Lantern Camp remains the start anchor.
- Lanternfall Hamlet added as a safer south-west secondary camp.
- Briar Watch added as a mid-map combat staging point.
- Rebuilt routes connect the start to all cave/dungeon landmarks.

Mob Spawns
