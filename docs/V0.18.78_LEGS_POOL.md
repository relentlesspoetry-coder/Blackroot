## Finding — save-persisted item snapshot (pre-existing; Roadmap Item 10)

While verifying, the preview's *auto-loaded dev world* showed a stale 302-item runtime catalog even
though `DR.ITEM_BY_ID` and `game.editorItems` held all 312. Root cause: `systems/world-serializer.js`
**persists `game.editorItems` into a saved world** (line ~395) and **restores it on load** (line
~530), so opening an older dev/editor world reinstalls that world's stale item snapshot and the
runtime catalog rebuilds from it. New gear (belts/bags/chest/legs) won't appear in such a world until
a migration re-syncs `editorItems` from `DR.ITEM_BY_ID`.

This is **not introduced by this version** and does not affect the item data — fresh sessions,
`DR.ITEM_BY_ID`, and the audit are always current, and a forced catalog rebuild produces all 10 legs
correctly. It is squarely **Roadmap Item 10 (Save Compatibility & Migration)** and is flagged there.
