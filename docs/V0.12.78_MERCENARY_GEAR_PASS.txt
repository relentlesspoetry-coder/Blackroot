Dream Realms V0.12.78 — Mercenary Gear Pass

Scope:
- Make mercenary equipment functional.
- Allow compatible inventory gear to be assigned to mercenary slots.
- Apply role-aware restrictions and stat bonuses.
- Keep merc equipment persistent through the existing save state.

Changed:
- entities/mercenary.js
- systems/mercenary-system.js
- Dream Realms V0.12.78.html
- VERSION.txt
- README.txt
- docs/MODULAR_HISTORY.txt

Merc gear slots:
- Main Hand
- Off Hand
- Head
- Chest
- Legs
- Feet
- Trinket

Role compatibility:
- Guardian: Fighter/Cleric-style martial gear.
- Field Cleric: Cleric/Druid-style healer gear.
- Arcane Adept: Summoner/Enchanter/Necromancer/Druid/Cleric-style caster gear.
- Blade Scout: Rogue/Bard/Fighter-style light melee gear.

Runtime behavior:
- Compatible bag gear appears in the merc gear window.
- Clicking compatible gear equips it to the correct merc slot.
- Existing slot gear is returned to the player's inventory when replaced.
- Remove buttons return merc gear to the player's bags if capacity allows.
- Merc stats recalculate immediately after gear changes.

Cascade status:
- No bottom-of-file override layer.
- No delayed repair layer.
- No monkey-patch wrapper.
