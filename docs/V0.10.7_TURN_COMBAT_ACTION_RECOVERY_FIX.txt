Dream Realms V0.10.7 - Turn Combat Action Recovery Fix

Owning-system changes only.

Updated systems/turn-combat-system.js directly to repair the turn loop.

Fixes:
- Enemy turns now tick down and resolve instead of leaving combat stuck in enemy phase.
- Player turns now remain actionable with a valid target selected.
- Turn combat no longer blocks camera rotation/zoom while active.
- Attack no longer depends on the real-time attack timer during turn combat.
- Attack advances toward an out-of-range target instead of silently failing.
- Ability casting auto-locks a turn enemy as target when needed.
- Turn combat validates victory/defeat and ends cleanly.

No cascade patching, no bottom-of-file hotfix layer, no setTimeout wrapper.
