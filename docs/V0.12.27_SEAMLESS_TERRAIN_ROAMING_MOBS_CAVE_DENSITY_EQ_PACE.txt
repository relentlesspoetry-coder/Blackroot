Dream Realms V0.12.27 — Seamless Terrain, Roaming Mobs, Cave Density, EQ Pace Rebalance

Terrain Rendering
- Removed the heavy per-tile stroke outline from drawTile().
- Added tileTerrainFamily(), terrainTransitionColor(), drawEdgeStrip(), and drawTerrainEdgeTransitions().
- Terrain edge strips now render only when adjacent visual terrain families differ.
- Reduced tile bevel alpha to avoid the stamped diamond-grid look.
- Reduced grass/dirt micro-detail frequency so ground details no longer repeat every tile.
- Added drawContinuousWaterSurface() as a connected water overlay pass.
- Game.render() now draws terrain first, then water surface overlays, then sorted objects/entities/effects/damage.

Mob Behavior
- Enemy entities now have patrol targets, patrol wait timers, and patrol radii.
- Out-of-combat enemies roam around their home point instead of standing still.
- Respawn resets patrol state.

Cave Density
- Cave room spawn budgets were increased to roughly triple the prior pass.
- openCellsNear() search budget was increased for cave placement.
- Minimum-distance spacing remains active so cave mobs spread through rooms instead of stacking.

Combat Pace
- Enemy HP multiplier was increased in the Enemy constructor.
- Player auto-attack damage was reduced to stretch fights toward EverQuest pacing.
