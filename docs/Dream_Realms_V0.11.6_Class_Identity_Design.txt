Dream Realms — Class Identity Design Version Target: V0.11.6

Class Roster

1.  Fighter Role:

-   Heavy-weapon melee DPS

Identity: - Slow, powerful melee attacks - Two-handed weapon
specialist - High burst physical damage - Armor breaking - Cleaves -
Bleeds - Execute-style finishers

Not a tank: - No taunts - No shield-focused identity - No threat
management

2.  Rogue Role:

-   Fast burst melee DPS
-   Poison specialist

Identity: - Quick attacks - Backstab - Critical strikes - Bleeds -
Poison damage over time - High mobility - Evasion

3.  Bard Role:

-   Support DPS

Identity: - Songs - Buffs - Debuffs - Group utility - Moderate personal
damage

4.  Cleric Role:

-   Primary healer
-   Holy support

Identity: - Direct heals - Group healing - Holy damage - Defensive
blessings - Protection magic

5.  Druid Role:

-   Nature caster
-   Hybrid sustain

Identity: - Nature damage - Roots - Thorns - Healing - Poison -
Wildlife-themed magic

6.  Summoner Role:

-   Primary pet-focused caster DPS

Identity: - Strongest pet class - Multiple summon types - Pet
management - Pet synergies - Arcane/elemental summons

7.  Necromancer Role:

-   Undead pet caster
-   Attrition caster

Identity: - Skeletons and undead pets - Disease - Damage-over-time -
Life drain - Mana drain - Bone and corpse magic - Pet-supported
sustained pressure

8.  Enchanter Role:

-   Crowd control caster

Identity: - Mezmerize - Slow - Debuffs - Mana manipulation - Control
effects - Pull management
