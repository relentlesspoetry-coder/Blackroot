Dream Realms V0.13.41 — Autonomous Party HUD + Smart Bot Personality Pass

SYSTEMS CHANGED
- Party capacity and party-order state continue to use the centralized six-member limit: one player plus five companions.
- The responsive party HUD renders six possible frames with name, class/role, level, health, mana where applicable, status effects, selection, death, meditation, combat, and current AI activity.
- Party-frame left click selects any living friendly member for healing and buffs. Bot/merc right click is limited to Inspect, Talk where supported, and Remove From Party.
- Bot AI remains autonomous. No Follow, Guard, Assist, Passive, Attack, Meditate, or Dismiss bot command panel, command dispatcher, saved stance, or command-target state is present.

AUTONOMOUS BOT BEHAVIOR
- Invite evaluation now checks shared party capacity, duplicate membership, bot availability, other-party membership, level range, and current tank/healer/support coverage.
- Existing class-role AI continues to own follow spacing, target choice, healing, buffing, protection, ranged/melee positioning, obstacle routing, combat, and safe recovery.
- Role-aware formation offsets keep tanks forward, healers and casters behind, support near the group, and melee damage on a flank without teleporting.
- Bot healer/support candidate collection and friendly spell targeting use the complete active party roster.

PERSONALITY AND CHAT BUBBLES
- Added short line banks for Fighter, Cleric, Druid, Bard, Summoner, Necromancer, Rogue, and Enchanter.
- Covered joining, invite refusal/full party, combat, kills, low health/mana, healing, buffing, protection, rare enemies, loot, meditation start/interruption, cave/dungeon entry, and player/bot death events.
- A central speech queue applies party-wide, per-bot, and per-event cooldowns with event priorities to prevent five-bot spam.
- Renderer-owned bubbles follow the speaking bot, stay above the nameplate, wrap and clamp to the viewport, then fade. Existing chat logging is reused where appropriate.

SAVE / LOAD COMPATIBILITY

- Old four-member saves load normally; stale saved capacity metadata cannot reduce the current cap.

- Existing meditation cleanup still removes stale rest/slow/path state during restoration.
