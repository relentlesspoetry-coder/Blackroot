Dream Realms V0.15.50 — Nameplate Anchor Stacking Fix Pass

Fix the world nameplate bug where bot/player/pet nameplates were no longer positioned cleanly above the correct actor and nearby bot plates overlapped each other.

- Reset nameplate placement list once per world render frame.

No gameplay/content changes:
