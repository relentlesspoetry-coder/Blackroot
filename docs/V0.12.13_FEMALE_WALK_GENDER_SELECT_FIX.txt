Dream Realms V0.12.13 — Female Walk Animation and Gender Select Fix

Diagnosis
- Character creation exposed a Nonbinary gender option that was not requested.
- Female character rendering could bypass or flatten the normal planted-step leg/foot animation path, causing sliding.

Fix
- Removed Nonbinary from character creation options and bundled UI/data text.
- Kept female models on the shared humanoid walk-cycle owner so moveBlend, stride, foot lift, and planted-step animation apply consistently.
