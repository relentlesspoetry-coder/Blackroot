Dream Realms V0.14.61 — Class Meditation Animation Revamp Pass

Summary
- Reworked meditation animation ownership so each playable class has a distinct meditation pose/effect package.

Implemented
- Bard: seated performance meditation with the lute held across the lap and slow song-note motes.
- Druid: grounded cross-legged nature communion with leaf orbit effects.
- Fighter: braced weapon-rest meditation with warm martial focus rings.
- Rogue: low stealth crouch meditation with shadow/teal haze and grounded hands.
- Cleric: kneeling prayer meditation with clasped hands and holy halo rays.
- Enchanter: levitating lotus meditation with charm rings and mind-wave effects.
- Summoner: ritual focus meditation with orbiting summon motes.
- Necromancer: hunched death-rite meditation with bone-focus posture and dark motes.

Technical Notes
- Meditation state remains owned by game.js.
- Animation timing/progress is exposed through HumanoidAnimationSystem pose data.
- Class meditation anchors are resolved in HumanoidBaseRenderer, with class overlays remaining in their owning renderers.
- Existing cancellation behavior for movement, combat/damage, and interruption remains intact.
