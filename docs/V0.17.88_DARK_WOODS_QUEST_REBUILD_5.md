# V0.17.88 — Dark Woods Quest Rebuild V (escort + Silk Web Approach)

**Type:** Content + engine (quests)
**Previous version:** V0.17.87
**New version:** V0.17.88

Adds the `escort` verb and the **Silk Web Approach** ([21]–[23]) — including Blackroot's first
**permanently losable** quest.

---

## New engine (`systems/quest-system.js`)

The **escort verb** (`updateEscortEncounters` / `startEscort` / `endEscort`):

- An `escort` task spawns a slow, low-HP follower at `from` when the player arrives there — a passive
  `Pet` (`command: 'follow'`, `temporaryPet: true`) that will **not** fight and **can** be killed.
- The player leads it to `to`. Reaching `arriveRadius` of `to` sets the task complete (ready to turn in).
- If the follower dies, the quest **fails permanently**: `applyQuestOnFail` runs `quest.onFail`
  (`setVariable survivor_dead=1`) so the quest never re-offers, the follower is despawned, and the
  downstream Quartermaster / [23] never appear. This is the design doc's "first quest you can permanently
  lose."

## Content

- **[21] Cut Them Down** (Iren / `npc_deepwood_surveyor`): discover the expedition camp — newly placed at
  the cave mouth (52,145): three tents (one shredded), a cold fire, six webbed cocoons — then cut open the
  6 `approach_cocoon`. Five hold the dead; the sixth is the survivor.
- **[22] The Fourth Expedition Member** (`autoOffer` at the camp after [21], `foundAtPoi`): **escort** the
  survivor from (52,145) home to (100,100). `onComplete: survivor_saved=1`, `onFail: survivor_dead=1`;
  turn in to Iren. `requiredConditions: survivor_dead < 1` so a failed run never re-offers.
- **[23] Anti-Venom Supply** (new `npc_silk_web_quartermaster` @ camp 104,104, `requiredConditions:
  questCompleted quest_fourth_member`): gather `herb_gloomcap_mushroom` + deliver `item_spider_silk_thread`;
  reward `item_antivenom`. Only offers if you saved her.

New assets: item `item_antivenom`; NPC `npc_silk_web_quartermaster`; the expedition-camp scene in
`world-system.js` `ensureDarkWoodsMicroLandmarks`.

## Verification (in-browser, port 8899)

- **[21]**: discovering the camp seeds the discover objective; the 6 cocoons cut and the quest completes.
- **[22] success**: auto-offers at the camp after [21]; the follower spawns and follows (`command:'follow'`);
  moving it to (100,100) completes the escort; turning in sets `survivor_saved=1` and **[23] becomes
  available**.
- **[22] failure**: killing the follower removes the quest, sets `survivor_dead=1`, despawns the follower,
  and it does **not** re-offer; **[23] stays locked**.
- No console errors.
