Dream Realms V0.10.6 - Terrain Step Effects
===============================================

Added terrain-tag-based movement effects emitted only on player step timing.

Implementation ownership:
- Terrain profile data lives in data/tiles.js.
- Movement triggers are called from systems/collision-system.js and the water movement branch in systems/swimming-system.js.
- Effect spawning/timing lives in systems/terrain-footstep-system.js.
- Drawing lives in render/effects-renderer.js.

Rules:
- Effects only trigger while the player is moving, grounded, alive, not meditating, not mounted, and not swimming/falling.
- Effects are step-timed by movement distance, not spawned every frame.
- Particle counts are low and short-lived.
- Tiny offscreen effects are culled before spawning.
- Water sets wetness carryover but does not spam footstep effects while swimming.

Current terrain mappings:
- Grass / underbrush: grass blade flecks.
- Dirt / camp: dust puffs.
- Forest floor: leaf scatter.
- Stone / ruins: pebble grit.
- Cave floor: gray ash dust.
- Water: shallow ripple profile for future wading and wetness carryover.
