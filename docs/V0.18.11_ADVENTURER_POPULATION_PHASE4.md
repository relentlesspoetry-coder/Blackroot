# V0.18.11 — Adventurer Population, Phase 4 (abstract simulation)

Fourth slice of the Persistent Adventurer Bot AI System (plan:
`docs/PLAN_ADVENTURER_BOT_AI.md`). Adventurers now **progress off-screen**: even
when you never see them, they gain levels, gold, and gear over the days.

## What Phase 4 delivers
A cheap **abstract simulation** that advances **dormant** (off-screen, non-squad,
non-materialized) adventurers over in-game time, per their daily activity:
- **XP → level-ups** (up to level 20), on an XP curve, faster for group raids.
- **Gold earned** while adventuring/crafting/gathering (soft-capped).
- **Gear-score creep** from finds, plus a floor bump on each level-up.
- **Reputation** from real deeds (bigger for group hunts/raids).
- **HP** recovered while resting in town, spent while adventuring (bounded 0.1–1.0).
- **Supply spend** at dawn: adventuring adventurers pay a small gold cost for
  food/potions before heading out (the "spend" side; mercs/repair come in Phase 5).

Gains scale by activity multipliers and are reduced to 30% while an adventurer is
still walking to the site (full rate once they arrive).

## Mechanics (`systems/adventurer-population-system.js`)
- Each `ACTIVITIES` entry gains `xpMult / goldMult / repMult / hpRate` sim rates;
  new `SIM` tuning block (`xpBase`, `goldBase`, `repBase`, `travelFactor`,
  `goldSoftCap`, `gearFindPerHour`) and `xpForAdventurerLevel(level)`.
- `updateAdventurerAbstractSim(dt)` — called in the frame update between the daily
  scheduler and the materialization bridge. It advances by the **in-game-hour
  delta** (from `getWorldTimeInfo`, clamped to 6h against paused-tab/day-skip
  jumps) and only touches dormant records: materialized adventurers progress
  through their live bot; squad records through the player's party.
- Supply cost applied in `rollAdventurerDailyActivities` for non-town activities.
- `/who` floors the now-fractional reputation for display.

## Verified (in-browser, controlled test, 0 console errors)
Driving the sim over 48 in-game hours:
- A large-group raider (Doran) went **L9→15**, **gold 145→663**, **gear 108→182**,
  **rep 30→38**, **HP 0.9→0.1** (clamped) — leveled, earned, geared, and took
  attrition.
- A resting adventurer (Sable) recovered **HP 0.4→1.0**.
- **Level cap (≤20)** and **HP bounds (0.1–1.0)** respected.
- A **squad** record and a **materialized** record were **untouched** by the sim
  (they progress through their live bots, not the abstract tier).

Rates are deliberately generous for testing visibility; real play varies activity
daily and only sims dormant records, so progression is gradual. All `SIM` constants
are one-line tunables (Phase 9).
