# V0.18.84 — Dark Woods Cloaks / Cape Armor (Roadmap Item 4 A)

The **cape** slot gets a full pool of 10 cloaks. Unlike the body slots, capes are the **light utility
slot** — cloth/leather only (no plate/chain), low armour, and profile-driven (movement / caster /
healer / agile / defensive) rather than the four-armour-type split. Same sourcing discipline: every
piece cross-checked by the boot obtainability audit.

Split: **5 cloth / 5 leather**; **2 white / 5 green / 3 blue**. Rarity scaling lifts effective armour
to ~5 on the blue leather. Utility stats use only real STAT_KEYS (speed, magicResist, critChance,
healingPower, spellPower, intellect, stamina). Two class-oriented pieces (Runespun Cloak for casters,
Warden's Warcloak for Paladin/Warden/Fighter/Cleric).
