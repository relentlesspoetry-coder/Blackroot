# V0.19.1 — Quest Dialogue Pass, Part 1 (Roadmap Item 1)

Item 1 requires a full authored dialogue pass over all 26 Dark Woods quests. This is the first slice:
enforce the roadmap's hardest, most objective rule — **no UI/fourth-wall terminology in spoken
dialogue** — fix every violation, and add a validator so it can't come back.

## The rule

> "No fourth-wall language, developer terminology, UI terminology, or character-breaking objective
> summaries inside spoken dialogue."

…while the **objective summary** is *"a concise, unambiguous record for the quest journal. Separate
from spoken dialogue."*

## The violation — 7 of 26 quests

Every one ended its spoken `inProgressText` with a control instruction:

| quest | giver | ended with |
|---|---|---|
| quest_light_the_way | Road Warden Cael | "…Press E at each dark post." |
| quest_well_remembers | Sister Liora | "…(press E at the well)." |
| quest_pilgrims_road | Sister Maelle | "…(press E at the shrine)." |
| quest_cut_supply_lines | Road Warden Cael | "…(press E at each)." |
| quest_order_of_stones | Selene Glasswhisper | "…(press E at each). A wrong stone summons wisps." |
| quest_cut_them_down | Deepwood Surveyor Iren | "…(press E at each)." |
| quest_offerings | Elder Thalen Mossroot | "…press E at the shrine with each in your bag." |

## The fix

All seven rewritten as in-character speech in each giver's established voice, still reminding the
player what remains (the roadmap's state-3 requirement), with zero UI terminology. For example:

> **Cael:** "The posts won't light themselves, and the dark isn't waiting on you. Walk the trail and
> coax back every lantern you can — each one is a step of road the woods don't own."

> **Iren:** "My people are at the cave mouth — six of them, wrapped and hanging. Cut every last cocoon
> down. I don't care what's still breathing inside. Just cut them down."

### Removing the hints is safe — verified

The game already teaches the interact verb in **system text**, which is where UI terminology belongs:

- `game.js:1571` (start-up tip): *"Press B for bags and M for the full map. **Press E near NPCs/chests/
  events/dungeon entrances**, F near water, G near resources, and C near crafting stations."*
- `quest-system.js:123 / 1348 / 1392`: *"Press E near a quest source."*

So no player is stranded — including in the onboarding quest, whose note says it teaches the interact
verb.

### Objective summary

`quest_order_of_stones`' task label carried the least information of the set, so the puzzle
restriction moved **into the journal record** rather than being lost with the rewrite:

`"Wake the stones in the right order"` → **`"Wake the 8 stones sunwise from the north stone"`**

## New validator — `validateQuestDialogue()`

Scans the four **spoken** fields (`offerText`, `inProgressText`, `completedText`, `beforeOfferText`)
of every quest for banned UI/fourth-wall/developer terminology (`press X`, click, hotkey, hud, ui,
menu, button, F-keys, esc, respawn, hp bar, xp, debug, placeholder, todo). **Task labels are
deliberately not scanned** — they're the journal record and may state conditions plainly.

Wired into `runValidationSuite` as a **hard-fail** `questDialogue` section; the boot log gains
`N dialogue issue(s)`.

## Verification

- `node --check` clean; JSON valid; **0 console errors**; suite **PASS**.
- Dialogue issues: **7 → 0** across 26 quests.
- **The validator provably works**: re-injecting `"press E"` into a quest is caught on the correct
  quest and field (`term: "press E"`, `field: "inProgressText"`), and restoring the line returns it to
  clean — so it isn't passing vacuously.

## Correction to an earlier assumption

I previously said Item 1 would clear the 7 registry descriptor warnings. Investigating, that's wrong:

- **5 are false positives.** `giver`/`turnIn` are validated against **NPC names only**, but *"Rurik the
  Fallen"* is a **mob** (`mob_bandit_leader`) on two `autoComplete` bounty/branch quests whose own note
  says *"Rurik is not a turn-in NPC"*; and `quest_fourth_member` `autoOffer`s at a POI and turns in to
  Iren, so it needs no NPC giver.
- **1 is real:** an event references the nonexistent quest id `quest_darkwood_first_steps`.
- **1 needs a look:** `dungeon_glooms_crypt` references zone `blackroot_catacombs` while its `zoneId`
  is `dark_woods`.

Left for a follow-up rather than silently "fixed".
