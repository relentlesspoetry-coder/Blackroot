Dream Realms V0.12.46 - Corpse Loot Interaction Fix / Weather Readability Pass

Fixed:
- Corpse looting now registers lootable corpses when enemies die.
- Pressing E opens the nearest lootable corpse bag within interaction range.
- Corpse lookup now checks a persistent corpse registry plus active enemy arrays.
- Interaction range increased to reduce missed E presses while standing on/near a corpse.
- Corpse Loot window now confirms it was opened from E.

Weather polish:
- Fog definitions are denser and more visible.
- Rain definitions are denser with stronger intensity values.
- Rain streaks are brighter, thicker, and longer.
- Rain curtain and splash overlays are stronger.
- Fog bands, ground haze, and visibility haze are stronger over the Dark Woods palette.

Implementation notes:
- No bottom-of-file hotfix or wrapper layer was added.
- Changes were made in owning systems: loot-system, combat-system, game input, and weather-system.
