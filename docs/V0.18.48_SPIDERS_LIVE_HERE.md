## 1. Egg / baby spiders are genuinely tiny now (bug fix)
> "the spiders that come out of the eggs are way too big they need to be tiny spiders."

**Root cause** (not a scale tweak — an actual cache bug): the spider sprite cache (`render/entity-renderer.js`) keyed by a coarse `xs/s/m/xl` bucket and **baked `modelScale` into one shared bitmap per bucket**. A 0.32 hatchling and a 0.5 skitterer both fell in `xs`, so whichever cached first won and **shrinking a baby's `modelScale` did nothing**.

**Fix**: the cache now keys by the **quantized `modelScale`** (0.1 steps: `'q' + round(ms*10)`), so each distinct size gets its own baked bitmap (only ~6 sizes are ever used). Then the egg hatchlings (`makeBabySpider`) dropped **0.46 → 0.32**, and the filler `baby`/`little` variants **0.46/0.66 → 0.34/0.5**. The showcase confirms the sizes are now distinct and the hatchling is ~¼ the skitterer.

## 2. Floor webs look like woven spider silk
> "The webs on the floor need to look like webs that are woven together and look like spider webs!"

`drawSilkWebCavernSurfaceDetails` (`render/terrain-renderer.js`) built the front lattice from **straight corner-to-corner links**. Because tile corners are shared, those tiled into the rigid geometric triangle grid you saw. Replaced with an **irregular woven cobweb**: threads run between **jittered interior anchors** (not the shared corners) and **sag** like real draped silk, so no repeating geodesic mesh forms; the woven **orb web** now appears on more tiles (threshold 0.5 → 0.42). The floor reads as organic spider silk.

## 3. Real-cave dressing — rocks & stalagmites
> "little rocks in the dungeon, we need stalagmite columns and smaller stalagmite placed around randomly… also some need to be covered in webs."

- `drawCaveRocks` — small rock/pebble scatters; `obj.webbed` drapes silk. Placed in every room (one plain scatter + one webbed).
- `drawCaveStalagmite` — `obj.size`: **small** (everywhere), **large** (open caverns, silk-draped), **column** (a floor-to-ceiling stalagmite + stalactite meeting in the middle, in the big caverns); `obj.webbed` drapes silk over any of them. Proper tapered rock with a light ridge, shaded facet, and limestone banding.

## 4. Giant spider nests
> "really really large spider nests with spiders inside them and tiny spiders crawling all over them."

`drawGiantSpiderNest` (+ new `drawTinySpider` helper) — a large domed silk mass with a **dark mouth** (a big spider lurking inside, **eyes glowing**), **egg sacs**, ceiling anchor strands, and **tiny spiders crawling all over it**. Placed (1 each) in the big open caverns and the brood/nest rooms (never the boss room). Royal floors get a pink variant.
